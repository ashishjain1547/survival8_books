converts: spjain, BCL

definitely join IIMB.

About GDs/PIs:
None of my GDs were great . All were average or just average+
PI was good at IIMB and spjain and average at IIMC and IIML.

A PIECE OF ADVICECAT ASPIRANTS, DONT MISS THIS)
IT IS NOT DIFFICULT TO GET INTO IIMs. I AM NOT SAYING THIS BECAUSE I GOT THRU BUT BECAUSE I NEVER DID MUCH WELL AT ANY STAGE OF SELECTION PROCESS.
MY CAT PAPER WAS QUITE MESSED UP AND WAS MUCH BELOW EXPECTATIONS. WAS ABOUT TO CRY AFTER THE PAPER......
BUT SOMEHOW CLEARED CUTOFFS IN ENGLISH AND DI AND GOT CALLS.
NOW COMES THE GD AND PI.
WAS JUST ABOUT AVERAGE IN ALL GDs AND HAD NOTHING GREAT TO TALK ABOUT IN PI. IN LUCKNOW PI, THERE WERE MORE "I DONT KNOW"s THAN "I KNOW" THOUGH QUESTIONS ASKED WERE NOT THAT DIFFICULT.
STILL AFTER CONVERTING ALMOST ALL THAT I ATTENDED, I AM SURE ABOUT ONE THING. JUST GOING THROUGH THE SELECTION PROCESS AND KNOWING EXACTLY WHAT THEY WANT TO TEST IN YOU IS MORE THAN SUFFICIENT TO GET THROUGH.

LAST BUT NOT THE LEAST:
THE WHOLE SELECTION PROCESS IS DESIGNED SUCH THAT ANYONE WITH AN AVERAGE APTITUDE BUT TREMENDOUS HARD WORK CAN GET THRU............

A special thanks to PG and all its members and moderators without whom this was not possible.

Hard work never killed anybody, but why take a chance
cheers
Akash
