Re: Profile of People who have Converted their IIM Calls! - 01-05-2008, 11:39 PM
My Profile :
X class : 89% CBSE
XII class : 84% CBSE
b.tech+m.tech dual degree 7.6/10 iit madras
Work ex : 34 months (software/sap labs)

CAT scores : 99.5X %ile overall (with 99.XX in DI, 97.XX in VA and 96.XX in QA)

Calls : ACLIK
Converts : ACLI
Rejects : K

IIML : Had an average GD (6/10) and sub 5 minute interview with no major questioning. Converted.

IIMI : Good GD(7/10) and a okayish PI(6/10). Converted.

IIMA : Horrible PI(3/10). Couldn't answer one branch question and the only math question asked!! Few work related and cricket Qs saved me i guess. Interview went around for 12-13 mins as against the average 20 mins. Converted.

IIMK : Horrible GD(0/10) Didn't speak at all!!. Horrible PI (1/10) didn't speak much here either!! I was asked to speak on any topic of my choice for 3 mins in interview because i didn't open my mouth in the GD. Rejected

IIMC : Bad GD for the whole group! We were stopped after 5 mins!! Bad summary too. PI was very vague with Qs on VLSI design and semiconductors(I am not an elec engg). Probably my clarity of goals saved me here. Converted.

Guys, only preparation I did for gd pis is to go through this particular thread and threads of last year interviews. It helps a lot. It helps you know what to expect in which interview and what are the things needed for a convert from each iim. If you have 2+ years experience, you can forget your math and branch related questions and get away with it.
