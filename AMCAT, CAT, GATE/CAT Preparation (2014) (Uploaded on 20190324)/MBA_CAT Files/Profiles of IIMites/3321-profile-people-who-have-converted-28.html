VA %ile: 97.5


GD/PI Calls: BLACKI

Converts: B, L ,I (I was waitlisted in B @ 11, which eventually got converted)
Joining B (although I have already paid 15k to L)

My 2 cents:
I realized that only thing which matters in GD/PI is your performance in interview. I have a very good extra-curriculars and just above avg. CPI of 7.2 with a workex close to 2 yrs.
My L gd was a nightmare, worse than what you can experience in your mock sessions, neverthless with a very good PI, I made it to L.
On the other hand, I had ideological differences (extreme right wing) with panel in case of IIMC PI, therefore even after a blasting performance in gd, I was not called.
In case of A, I guess they look for a picture-perfect interview performance, which I couldnt deliver as I was too intimidated by the brand IIMA.
More importantly, always believe in watever decision you have made in life and never ever curse the system.

GD/PI Calls: BLACKI

Converts: A, K, I and MDI, SPJ, IIFT
What I've Learnt:
Mainly for someone like me, the odds are not very good. If you're an engineer and a fresher and a guy, just hope ur from one of the IITs or atleast an NIT. If you dont have any of these, then the only thing that can get you a top 3 convert is exemplary extra-curriculars, which I had thankfully.
Other than that, cracking CAT or any other test is not even half the work done. The GD/PI stages are the real clinchers. As popular opinion goes, the interviews are the most important phase, and GDs are hardly watched i feel.
