Adios,

ThA HAaa

PS:

Some key take aways from this entire experience..
  • A good CAT score is never too harmful. A balance in the scores is always well appreciated.
  • In a GD - Be seen as someone who is gainfully engaging in a discussion rather than someone who is trying to blow someone else's head off. Air time never sells but the quality of your point does. .The best thing about a GD is the amount of time one has to think. Think haaard and make only one point - but make a killing point !
  • In the interview - honesty pays, Cheerfullness pays, and humour pays BIGG time. Being tense will hardly solve matters. Know what you have done well and dont bother about the rest..which...even GOD doesn't care...leave along these IIM professors
  • Work experience always helps. Try to make sure it is shown and portrayed in the right mannner. And for GOD's sake dont mess up questions related to your work ex. That is invariably a one way trip to the rejection drum !!

KABOOM
