GD/Interviews were all straightforward, in the sense, that I only focussed on my strong points and found a few ways to direct the interview in those directions whenever possible. Wish you guys all the best for your journey to an MBA! Give it your best shot, but never compromise with your current job! CAT is an exam, which requires some very BASIC skills and knowledge. The most critical factor on the day of the exam is your coolness factor. If you put your job in jeopardy, chances are that you will be highly stressed on the D-day. That will like losing half the battle! Keep your approach simple and stress-free, and you're gonna luv it!!

1. 2. CAT is a dicey test..Even if you prepare well, you might end up with low scores.. Be sure to have backup options
