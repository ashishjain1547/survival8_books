Interviews and my perception:

L: Excellent GD, Excelled PI, Was dead sure on getting out for conversion. Did convert

K: Excellent GD, Below avg PI, Was hopeful on conversion bcoz of high work exp and fact that K has big waitlist otherwise PI was bad. Did convert

C: Pathetic GD, Avg Interview. Was dead sure wont convert. And didnt convert. It was one bad day when lots of bad things happened

A: Good GD, Good PI, was hopeful of conversion. Did convert.
One thing about A was that they have a detailed form for PI and i spent a lot of time on it. If i think abt total time spent it wud be atleast around 30-40 hrs. I think I did a good justice to the written form. I believe it was what tilted things in my favour.


as of others:
NITIE: Below avg GD, Excellent PI. was hopeful of conversion. Waitlisted at 15. So will convert
MDI: Bad GD. Bad PI. Was sure of reject. Got a waitlist (287)

I believe that gut feeling on getting out of venue is a good judegment. Atleast when u r sure of reject and if ur both GD PI went well then u will surely convert.

Process seems to be very objective with different parameters like work-ex, acads , extra curriculars having different weightages across different institutes

There is not much you can do in a GD if you land up in with lots of talkative and aggressive ppl who are jsut not willing to listen and they can take the whold grp down with them. Something that happened with me in C in which mods interrupted twice for maintaining decorum (all bcoz of two candidates)
PI is where you can play upto your strength and direct the course by dropping off specific hints for the profs to pick up.
