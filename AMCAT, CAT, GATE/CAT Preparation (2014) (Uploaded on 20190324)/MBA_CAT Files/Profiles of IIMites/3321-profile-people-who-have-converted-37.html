10th - 85%

B.tech EEE from NIT Trichy CGPA - 7.23

CAT 2006 (3rd attempt)
QA-97.44
Verbal-97.14
DI-97.82
Overall-99.26

CALLS-ACLIK

A
Started off the GD...Everyone was looking at me nodding their heads hearing my points...Would rate it as a more than a decent performance
PI wasn't all that great...started off well...but later fumbled on some simple concepts like per capita income

C
Started the GD here too....gave a few points...couldn't summarise properly....would rate it as a just about okay performance
PI was bad....was a little nervous...dunno why....

L
This was my first GD among the IIMs....so didn't speak much though i dropped in one or two points...overall not all that satisfactory...
PI started off bad...had made a mistake while entering my salary though i fended it off well...had a real decent interview after that

I
Should say that this was my best ever performance though the table we all were sitting in was so narrow that I had to reach out real forward to see the other guys and as a reason the moderators could see only my back
Interview started with technical questions...it became apparent that i didn't know a thing....so the interviewer moved on to my hobbies and all which i answered pretty well

K
Worst ever GD in my life....wasn't allowed to finish a sentence due to certain overzealous ppl
Was asked in my interview on why i didn't speak during the GD...told them that i was conserving my energy for the interview ...the interview after that consisted of a rapid fire set of technical questions which left me in a daze....the interviewers were certainly jolly and they gave me a sincere all the best for the remaining interviews...i had a smile on my face when i left the hall but i had no hopes of converting it

Final Verdict
Rejects - A,C,L
Converts - K
Waitlists - I

So you see folks....it's not always that things go as you predict
