A - decent essay, superb pi. had a "euphoric" feeling after giving the pi. u can check my signature for the pi.
B - avg gd, good pi. dunno how i got rejected.
C - avg gd, not-so-good pi. was a pure HR interview, messed up things.
L - good gd, decent pi. rejection was a touch harsh.
I - best gd of my life, best pi as well. absolutely shocked as to how couldnt convert this one.
K - poor gd, average pi. shocked at conversion.

i think contrary to low gd weightage, it does play some role. & i think PI has a weightage far far more than what is stated. and i definitely think that this time around, NOT being an engineer helped. even they want a diverse background, and nearly all the ppl whom i met had an engineering background. finally, i think ppl should relax as much as possible. i was tensed before my C pi, and hence messed it up. i grew extremely frustrated, and played like hell on my comp for the next 2-3 days (i had C on 14th march morning and A on 17th march afty). didnt prepare anything for my A pi separately. was absolutely chilled out on the day of A pi, while others were supertensed. thr were 5 of us from kgp, and we were laughing and cracking jokes, while the others were staring at us as if we
were aliens . so it definitely helps if u r relaxed.
