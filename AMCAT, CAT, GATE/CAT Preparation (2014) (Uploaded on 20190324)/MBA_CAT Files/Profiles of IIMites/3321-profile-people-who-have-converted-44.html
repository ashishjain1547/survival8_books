My 2 paise on GDPI:

1) Be yourself. Please do not fake anything, anything at all. Its ok if your hobby is watching Govinda movies, its ok if you don't read, its ok if you are coming to IIM for a great job, & not for enterprenuership/personal development et al. Though its not ok if your definition of a great job is just great money & nothing else.
2) Know yourself. You don't need to know all the GK the coaching guys tell u, you just need to know a little bit about your city, your college, your company etc.
3) Smile! Appear calm & keep smiling all through the interview, no matter what is happening.

Also, PI is the real thing, You can always thoroughly mess up your GD, & get through.(I spoke just once in my C GD). Though you won't get through if you are excessively rude in the GD.
