IIM Final Call - 05-05-2006, 08:01 PM
Hi all,
Feels like heaven to finally post here.
First of all my profile-:
B.Tech(hons) in Agricultural and Food Engineering from IIT Kharagpur. CGPA-8.08.
22 months work-ex in Infosys.
extracurricular just ok.some positions here and there.

Appeared fr CAT thrice. 2 serious attempts( once in Final year..serious attempt , last yr and this year(again a very serious one))....
Had BLACKI Calls(99.27 with a decent distribution)
Converts only I...waitlisted @ L ...99.99% going to I.

Fundaes gained-:
1. I took test series of TIME for all the three years. I didn't do very well in any one of them but always believed that i could do well on the actual day( my actual percentile was 99.27, not very gr8..but ok i suppose). My best rank was 121 or so in TIME. I felt the same in IITJEE prep also. I never ever got good ranks in those also, but definitely made it into IIT. So,please don't get frustated if you are not able to do well in simcats etc.
This is not to say of course that doing well is not good, but don't be puzzled if you are not able to do so. Having said all this, please give the simcats very seriously without putting much brains to the results.If it good ok, if not still ok.
2. Next is about the work/study juggling act. I have seen many ppl make a helluvah abt job pressure, how do i study etc. I was in a project which demanded a lot...and a lot means a lot..i used to spend...13-14 hrs a day in office. Sometimes on weekdays i cudn't study also. But what i always think abt CAT..it is more abt orientation more than anything else. I was always focussed and that's what helped i suppose. Though i didn't actually do well in the GD/PI phase but i did do well in the written exams, without actually taking any leave at all!!
I just made it a point to be out of office on weekends. that was easy. So, the whole funda is doing the right thing at the right point. That's it.

Lastly i wud thank pagalguy and all the pagals fr sharing their funda etc which was very helpful to say the least.

That's all.
Thank you.
