Hi All,
This is a great thread and really dreamt of posting what I am going to post…

My Profile

Academics:

X – 86.4 % (CBSE)
XII – 82.4% (CBSE)
B.Tech (Chem.)– 83.2 % (B.I.E.T Jhansi U.P.)
M.Tech (Chem.-Process Engg. & Desgin)– 8.9/10 (I.I.T. Delhi)

Work Experience:

RIL Jamnagar refinery (Technical Dept.) – 8 months
HPCL Mumbai Refinery (Projects + Tech Dept.) – 12+ months (still continuing…)

CAT: 99.79 %

DI – 99.61%
Verbal – 97.97%
QA – 97.15%

Calls: BLACKI

Experience (and interesting highlights):

20th Feb: IIM K (Mumbai)
GD – (10 min 9 people) – Good - though in terms of content came three times with ok points but good in terms of flow was able to enter and sustain at will. Psyched people in a semi fish market by getting airtime at will got a complement after GD that I was the best from a fellow participant
Interview – Got cracking – took them to my strength areas and guided the interview.
Result – IIM K is happy to give me an Admit

7th March: IIM L (Mumbai)
Essay – V. Good – Covered all points that was later discussed in GD
GD – (8 minus 1 people 14 min) – Good – though came many times but group feeling was absent it was kind of 1 vs 3. As one guy hell bend on few points and we 3 want to diversify. Still did manage to do a good job, flow and content both where flowing.
Interview – average – try to take them to my strength had a good discussion on my strengths and after that the panel said ok lets now start the interview. (Probably pressed the strength button too soon). A very objective interview answered few - and missed few (2 to be exact).
Result - IIM L is happy to give me an Admit

10th March: IIM A (Delhi)
Case Study (8 minus 1 people and 15 min) – Excellent / Flawless – 1 second break no one started – I gave a good start – explained the problem and moved on – gave direction and structured the discussion - analyzed various angles to problem and solutions from different perspectives. Tonality + Physiology + Content all of them worked for me.
Interview: Got cracking - First one to be interviewed in Delhi. Interview on my strengths. Interviewer where really nice and friendly people, I was able to build a rapport.
Highlights: While I was leaving…
Interviewer: Take a toffee
Me: Oh! So these are famous IIM Ahmedabad toffee.
Interviewer: But you will not eat it? (As I have written In my form I don’t take sweets - under weight loss program)
Me: I will keep it as a souvenir
Interviewer: Oh so you will not eat it? (with anger and surprise)
Me: No I will eat it when I come to IIM Ahmedabad for admission.
Result: IIM A gave me an opportunity to have the toffee at their Gate.

14th March: IIM C (Mumbai)
GD – (10 people and 15 min)Very Good – A tough topic – waited for 2 seconds no one started so I started – Smooth start – bisected the topic and presented it beautifully – Chip in with good points – Abstract topic so came up with different viewpoints.
Interview: Got cracking - If rapport building is an Art then I and interview panel where great artist on the D day. Tonality + Physiology + Content + Presence of mind work to great effect for me. Interview to my strengths. Great conversation I was giving witty answers and they appreciated it. I really enjoyed them and probably they enjoyed me.
Highlights: Only one question was asked rest all was conversation on my profile
I (Alumni): Give three good reasons why we shouldn’t select you?
Me: If they are good you will not select me.
I (Alumni): that’s the risk you run?
I (Alumni2): You can evade the question the question if you want?
Me: I have a Veto?
I (Alumni2): No you don’t have Veto you will just evade the question?
Me: I don’t evade?
I (Alumni): Then Answer
Psyched them with my first answer.
Second answer was well taken
Third answer goes as: If I get all 6 admits then I have to select between A/B/C. if I don’t select C – C should be humble enough to except it. If I select C – C should be proud of it.
I (Alumni): Thank you wit a smile. You may go now.
Stopped by one guy (probably a Phd student and asked questions on Mathematics as I had a RMO certificate
(Had some more funny moments during the interview)
Result: IIM C is a humble institute. (Thanks IIM C)

18th March: IIM I (Mumbai)
Case Study (10 people and 15 min): Very Good – waited for 3 seconds no one started so I started – Defined the problem – analyzed it – gave solutions and some out of box ones too. Got a complement after GD that I was the best from a fellow participant.
Interview: - Got Cracking – Cracked it big time – Mix of Techie. + current affair + G.K. + history and Numerical methods. Cracked all questions.
Results: IIM I is happy to give me a Admit. (Thought they goofed up with DOB – but I am inJ)

23rd March: IIM B (Mumbai)
Case Study (6 minus 1 people – 15 mins): Ok start + came in superb form during the process – waited for 4 second no one started so I started. Same usual process of case study. But was able to come up with detail points with cause-effect analysis. Tonality + Physiology + Content was excellent after the start.
Interview: Got Cracking – they were having lunch and interviewed me. Didn’t even offer me anythingL. Even I was hungry 3:30 P.M. Interview totally on my strengths. I was able to build excellent rapport. I enjoyed them and probably they enjoyed me.
Highlights: had few if interested PM me.
Result: IIM B Thanks for the congratulations message you typed against my name.

My strengths:
Acads
Work Ex
Opinion based question
Weight loss (lost 25 kgs in 8 months)
Power of now

So this is my journey of IIM

Converts: BLACKI

(Well we all know where i am going)

Future aspirants please feel free to contact me if required.

Regards,
Adi
