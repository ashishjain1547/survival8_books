Ascent's Group Discussion and Personal Interview Classes
Ascent's GD and PI contact classes commences on February 11, 2003. The classes are held only in the city of Chennai

Cracking the written tests of some of the best B Schools in the country is a feat in itself. However, that is just one of the most difficult stages that you have crossed in your race to securing a seat in the IIMs. The next two stages are equally important. Having, come this far as cracking the written test, it would be a highly undesirable outcome if one does not convert the call into an admission.

Most aspirants do not start their preparation for Group Discussions and Personal Interviews until after receiving the call letters from the IIMs and other top business schools. And, in many cases, it might just be a little too late. So, if you are keen on cracking CAT, it is time you got cracking with your GD - PI preparation.

Ascent's GD-PI Course Details
  • Structure of the course
    Refresher Course on Concepts
    The preparation for the GD-PI at Ascent starts with a crash course on various Economic, Business and Technology topics. While most of us know that one's group behaviour skills is one of the important aspect that is evaluated in a GD, what many of us are unaware is that contextual familiarity and conceptual clarity are given equal, if not more importance, in the evaluation. The refresher is aimed at getting you acquainted to relevant content. A partial list of the topics covered include
    • Introduction to GDP, GNP, Fiscal Deficit and related topics
    • Refresher in foreign exchange rate, forex reserves, convertibility and related topics
    • Refresher on economic reforms in India and brief overview of how various sectors in the economy have been opened up since 1991.
    • Overview of commonly used banking terms like CRR, SLR, PLR, bank rates.
    • Overview of how the stock market works and refresher on GDR, ECB, ADR and derivatives trading
    • Overview of the recent corporate scams such as Enron, Worldcomm, Arthur Andersen and the Sarbanes Oxley Act
    • Refresher on Telecom sector liberalization in India and a comparison of technologies such as CDMA, GSM, GPRS, Wireless LAN
    Mock GDs
    The refresher course is followed by Mock GDs. Each participant will have the opportunity to participate in adequate number of GDs to help him/her overcome all misgivings in GDs. Individual and group feedback sessions follow, to help you hone your skills.
    Personal Interviews
    Each participant will go through a series of interviews (minimum 3) and will be interviewed by a panel comprising graduates from top B Schools who are presently working in various capacity in the Indian industry. Individual feedback will be provided and progress monitored over the course of the series of interviews.
    In addition, those candidates who have got calls from top b schools can have mock interviews scheduled a few days before their actual interview date to help them gain confidence and hone their answers.
  • Course Material
    Study material covering basic dos and donts in a GD and Personal Interview and a list of topics that have appeared in the past in the GDs of IIMs and top B Schools with pointers on what to discuss in some of the important topics will be provided. In addition, the study material will also contain a list of questions asked in the personal interviews in CAT in the past.
  • Faculty
    The faculty comprises graduates from top B Schools such as the IIMs with industry experience.
