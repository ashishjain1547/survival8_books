WordKiller Help

 

The core idea of WordKiller is to "kill" a word by changing its color from black to gray. :)

What about WordKiller's functions ?

1 . Each word in Rich Text has one of the three colors: Black, Gray and Blue
2 . Kill a word you known in Rich Text by double-clicking it
3 . Transform word list text (one word per line ) to formated Rich Text
4 . Read articles in Computer Based Test Font (CBT Font) (How to install CBT Font ?)
5 . Print your own words list
6 . Work with WordHacker Export function together

How to use WordKiller ?

1. Open a RichText (*.rtf) file
2. Double click a word to change its color

or

1. Open a word list text file (one word per line) -- You can export a word list from WordHacker !
2. WordKiller will transform it to RichText format
3. Save it as RichText word list file
4. Double click a word to change its color in turn of Black, Gray and Blue

or

1. Open a RichText word list file
2. Print the word list
