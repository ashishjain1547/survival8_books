I. Ford on the Road

Add some josh to your life as you check out your knowledge on one of the world's most famous automobile brands: Ford. (4 pts each)

  1. Identify the odd one, out of the following brands: Volvo, Mazda, Lincoln, Saturn, Jaguar.



  2. Ford Motors produced its first car in 1903. What was the car known as?



  3. The Volkswagen model VW Beetle became the highest sold individual car model in 1973, beating the earlier record sales of a Ford model. Name the Ford sub-brand which earlier held that record.



  4. One of the greatest flops in the history of new brand launches, this car model named after Henry Ford's son created a loss of over $800 million in the late 1950s. Name the model.



  5. Some years ago, Maruti Udyog advertised achieving cumulative sales (in over 15 years) of 1 million vehicles of Maruti 800 in the country. Which year did Ford Motors first achieve annual sales exceeding one million, and which model was responsible for such whopping sales?



II. BRAND PLAY

Rosser Reeves once warned advertisers: ``An advertiser can be running two different campaigns and not know it, the campaign he thinks he's running and the campaign the consumer sees.'' This stresses the importance of a single proposition in any ad. Check out the following sanitary napkin brands and match them with the companies that own/market them. (1 pt each) 

    1. Kotex                       a. Christine Holden
2. Stayfree                    b. Kimberly Clark
3. Whisper                     c. Johnson & Johnson
4. Wonder Wings                d. Procter & Gamble
5. Comfit                      e. Gufic
