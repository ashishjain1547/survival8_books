I. IN THE NEWS

Check your knowledge on the business of newspapers. (4 pts each)

  1. The Times of India has been recently ranked the world's No.1 English daily newspaper in terms of circulation. Which newspaper ranks No.2 on the list?



  2. There are three Indian newspapers in the top 10 highest circulated English dailies in the world. The Times of India is one. Name the other two.



  3. With which newspaper would you associate the names Charles Dow and Edgar Jones - who also founded the famous stock market index?



  4. It was the famous voice of the Russian communist government until it closed down in 1991. Name the newspaper.



  5. All the President's men was a famous book and movie based on two journalists' experience while uncovering the Watergate scandal in the 1970s. Which newspaper did they work for?
