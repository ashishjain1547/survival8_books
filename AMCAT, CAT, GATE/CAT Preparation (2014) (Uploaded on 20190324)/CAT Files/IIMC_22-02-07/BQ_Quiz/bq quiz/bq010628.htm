Brand Quest      (June 28, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. GO MOBILE

Check out how much you know about India's mobile phone service providers. (4 pts each)

  1. Name the cellular service provider in Chennai in which the Thapar company Crompton Greaves has a minority stake?



  2. Which company launched the first mobile phone operation in India, and in which city?



  3. What do the following telecom acronyms stand for a. CPP b. WLL c. WAP



  4. Which brand has replaced Modi Telstra in the Kolkata market following withdrawal of the Australian company Telstra?



  5. Although Hutchison Telecom markets the brand Orange in Mumbai, this brand is owned by the company which has a 26 per cent stake in BPL Mobile. Name the international company which owns the brand.



II. BASELINE PLAY

Check out the following baselines and match them with the technology brands that use them. (1 pt each) 

    1. `Expect great things'                   a. Oracle
2. `Software powers the Internet'          b. Silicon Graphics
3. `The Software that manages eBusiness'   c. Alcatel
4. `One Step Ahead'                        d. Computer Associates
5. `Architects of an internet world'       e. Lucent Technologies



III. COPY TEST

Following are words picked out of the body copy of a print ad, voice-over of a TVC or simply a baseline. Identify the brand. (3 pts each)
  1. `The world might envy Mr And Mrs Raina's sense of fashion. They themselves are proud of their sense of economics'



  2. `Where does your wardrobe fall short? In between sizes'



  3. `Be good to yourself. Fly _____'



  4. `Some people wonder, ``Is there life out there?'' We wonder, ``How many bearings do they need?'''



  5. `As comfortable as wearing nothing at all'
