I. Brandstand

Check your knowledge on some famous international brands :

  1. Known as the inventor of waterproof clothing, he is also credited with creating a new cotton garment called Gaberdine, a name which he coined based on Shakespeare writing. He has given his name to a famous brand. Name the brand.



  2. At the age of 62, he sold his vineyard to invest in a golf equipment manufacturer in 1982. In less than a decade his name became synonymous with a premium golf gear. Name this eponymous brand.



  3. Launched in 1921, it was the first perfume brand ever to bear a couturiers name. Name this brand which derives its sub-brand name from the creators favourite number .



  4. In 1837, a 14_year_old boy arrived in Paris and soon gained fame with elegant designs of trunks, and was hired by Empress Eugenie as a personal dress-packer. His name has become a renowned premium brand in travel and leisure luggage industry. Name the brand.



  5. What famous brand of Havana cigars derives its name from a character in a Alexander Dumas classic who was a cigar connoisseur ?



II. BASELINE PLAY

More than three decades ago , David Ogilvy advised clients: "Do not compete with your agency in the creative area. Why keep a dog and bark yourself?" The common reason cited by most agencies for poor ads is,``that's what the client wanted''. Clients dictate the creative process, often creating many disconnecting elements in the ad, particularly baselines, which normally stick out of an ad without a connection! Check out the following baselines and match them with the mutual funds that use them : 

    1.  Adding value to your investments            a.   LIC Mutual Fund
2.  Continuing a tradition of trust             b.   Kothari Pioneer
3.  We understand what you're working for       c.   HDFC Mutual Fund
4.  Helping you get what you want               d.   IL & FS Mutual Fund
5.   With you, all the time                     e.   IDBI Principal
