Brand Quest      (September 28, 2000)

I. Headlines

Check your knowledge of some well known international advertising headlines. (4 pts each)

  1. What was advertised with the famous baseline: `Don't leave home without it!'?



  2. What was the famous advertising line of Braniff Airways created by George Lois, which subsequently became a catchphrase for an entire generation in the US?



  3. `You are in a beauty contest every day in your life', was the famous adline for a brand of toilet soap. Name the brand.



  4. Complete the brand in the following adline: `_____ refreshes the parts other beers cannot reach'.



  5. What brand mirrored the social change of a whole generation in the 1960s with the famous adline: `You've come a long way baby!'?



II. BRAND PLAY

The sociologist Marshall McLuhan once wrote: ``Historians and archaeologists will one day discover that the ads of our time are the richest and most faithful reflections that any society ever made of its entire range of activities.'' In many categories with undifferentiated products, the advertising and the imagery hold the key to the consumer choice. Match the following brands of lap-top computers with the company that markets them. (1 pt each) 

    1. Powerbook                a. Toshiba
2. Think Pad                b. Apple
3. Armada                   c. Hewlett Packard
4. Tecra                    d. Compaq
5. Omni                     e. IBM
