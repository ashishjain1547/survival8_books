II. BASELINE PLAY

Leo Burnett once said, ``The work of an advertising agency is warmly and immediately human. It deals with human needs, wants, dreams and hopes. Its `product' can't be turned out on an assembly line.'' How much does advertising reflect the relevant human needs as its theme, particularly in categories like credit cards? Check out the following baselines and match them with the credit card brands that use them. (1 pt each) 

    1. `The Power to give'                  a. HSBC
2. `Your world of financial services'   b. Citibank
3. `Chaandi ho gayee'            c. ICICI
4. `People. Not Plastic'                d. American Express
5. `Do more'                            e. Tata Finance



III. COPY TEST

Following are words picked out of the body copy of a print ad, voice-over of a TV commercial, or simply, a baseline for the brand. Can you identify the brand advertised?
  1. `For hands-free comfort'



  2. `Iske saath muffler free de rahe hain na?'



  3. `Kabhi health, kabhi fun'



  4. `Sell things to buy things you really need. Log on to ____'



  5. `dekhte dekhte do lakh'

  1. Name Japan's largest cellphone operator which is expected to be the first in the world to commercially roll-out 3G network services ?

    • Sony

    • Michael Jackson
