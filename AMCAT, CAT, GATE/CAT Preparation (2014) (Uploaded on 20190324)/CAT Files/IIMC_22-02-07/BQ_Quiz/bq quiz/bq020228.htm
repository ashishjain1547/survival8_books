I. THE LOGO IN QUESTION

Check your knowledge on some of the worlds famous logos :

  1. Which company derives its logo from decorative oriental sea shells ?



  2. What famous cigarette derives its name as well as its famous icon from an animal known for its existence in the deserts ?



  3. What animal would find in the tailwing of the Qantas aircrafts ?



  4. What animal appears in the monogram of Peugeot cars and has been registered as the trademark of the company ?



  5. Citibank has recently changed its logo in India - adopting its international logo after its merger with the Travelers Group. What is the logo difference ?

  4. Kabhi bhi Kaise bhi its all about loving your stomach
