Brand Quest      (May 24, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. Pot Pourri

A mixed bag of questions this week. (4 pts each)

  1. Which company has recently acquired the famous hair colourant brand Clairol from Bristol Myers Squibb?



  2. The Times of India group has recently launched a magazine targeted at men titled IAGT. What does IAGT stand for?



  3. Who Wants to be a Millionaire catapulted ABC to the top of the charts in TV viewership over the last 2 years. Which blockbuster TV show has placed CBS on top of the charts recently?



  4. Eugene Cernan was recently in the news, and also came to India as the brand ambassador for Omega watches. What is his claim to fame?



  5. It was recently announced that an international pizza chain is planning to rename itself San Marzano since its current name suggested that it was in the fast food business. Name the chain.
