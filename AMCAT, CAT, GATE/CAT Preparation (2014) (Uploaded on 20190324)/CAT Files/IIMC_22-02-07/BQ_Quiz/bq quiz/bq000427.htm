I. THERAPY TIME

Here's a dose of questions on over-the-counter drugs. (4 pts each)

  1. Which company owns famous OTC brands Benadryl and Actifed?



  2. This famous pain-killer was introduced in 1933 by Roche and is currently marketed in India by Nicholas Piramal. Name the brand.



  3. Which company markets Anacin in India, and which company owns this brand internationally?



  4. What famous brand was recently sold by Duphar Interfran to SmithKline Beecham Consumer Healthcare for an estimated Rs.40 crores, one of the highest prices paid for an OTC brand?



  5. The trademark of Bayer, this international brand has been a benchmark in OTC advertising, and has been represented by boy icon `Speedy' since the 1950s. Its Web site is described as the 'the most effervescent place in the world'. Name the brand.
