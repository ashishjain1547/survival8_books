I. JAPAN CALLING

Check your knowledge on some famous Japanese brands. (4 pts each)

  1. Which famous company started as Asahi Optical in 1919 - before adopting its current name in 1957? (This famous brand is based on the new technology it introduced that year.)



  2. What is the origin of the famous brand Epson?



  3. What brand name was given , in 1924, by the Mayor of Tokyo when he released the first watch produced by Shokosha Watch Company?



  4. Which famous Japanese brand does Aishwarya Rai endorse (for its ads in India)?



  5. In the world of Japanese business, who or what is 'Jetro' ?
