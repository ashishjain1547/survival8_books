Brand Quest      (June 27, 2002)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. POTPOURRI

A mixed bag of questions this week. (4 pts each)

  1. Acer Communications & Multimedia, a subsidiary of Acer Corporation, recently relaunched itself under a new, curious brand name, with a brand campaign with the tagline 'Enjoyment Matters'. Name the brand.



  2. Who recently said, and in what context, "Steve's the No.1 guy and I'm the No.2 guy ... I have a strong voice, a strong recommendation, but Steve has to decide"?



  3. Hindustan Lever recenly launched ready-to-cook chapatis under the Annapurna brand. The product description for the brand is not mere 'chapatis' but an interesting variant including the core customer proposition in the descriptor. What am I referr ing to?



  4. What's common to the following brands: Vodafone, Shell, Bridgestone, Marlboro, Fiat?



  5. Pepsi and Coca-Cola are using famous brothers from the cine industry to promote rival brands. In which market are they doing this, and name at least one of the two famous brothers.



II. Brand Match

III. COPY TEST

  2. '1 degree of separation between my company and your company'



  3. 'Forget our sales records. Let others match our new car'



  4. 'This clearly is the most well-built luxury car. Only 999 other ____ can claims the same.'



  5. 'Life is our Life's work'
