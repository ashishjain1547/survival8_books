I. THE STORYBOARD

Check your knowledge on some of the current TV commercials and their storylines. (4 pts each)

  1. In which current TV commercial would you encounter a teenager squirming trying to gain courage to buy an adult movie at a video store ?



  2. Which series of TV commercials has two characters as commentators constantly discussing on the various terms of cricket - a format which the brand has adopted for more than 2 years over more than half a dozen commercials ?



  3. Which current TV commercial featuring ShahRukh Khan is actually a teaser ad - with a 'What happens next' ending asking viewers to log onto a website for the rest of the story ?
