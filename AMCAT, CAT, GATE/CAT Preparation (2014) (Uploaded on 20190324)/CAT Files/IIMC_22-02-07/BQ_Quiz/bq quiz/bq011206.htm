I. POT POURRI

A mixed bag of questions this week. (4 pts each)

  1. What term, used rather frequently of late, was first used by economist Alfred Sauvy to describe countries that do not have sophisticated industrial economies?



  2. In the history of the technology business, who or what was `ENIAC'?



  3. Which famous business executive's autobiography is titled Swimming Across: A memoir?



  4. Which famous brand - almost generic to the category - and owned by Reckitt Piramal is in the news for a planned takeover by Morepen Labs?



  5. In which city did the famous `Fourth Ministerial conference' of the World Trade Organisation (WTO) recently take place?



II. MATCH PLAY

The country of origin plays a big role in driving a brand's personality and the culture of an organisation. Check out these famous international brands and match them with their country of origin. (1 pt each) 

    1. Ericcson           a. Japan
2. Benetton           b. Sweden
3. Heineken           c. USA
4. Bridgestone        d. The Netherlands
5. Haagen-Dazs        e. Italy
