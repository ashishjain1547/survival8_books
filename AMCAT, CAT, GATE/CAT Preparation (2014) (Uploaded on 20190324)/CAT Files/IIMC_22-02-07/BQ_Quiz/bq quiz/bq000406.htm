  1. Which famous home appliances manufacturer was originally founded by the great inventor Thomas Alva Edison more than 100 years ago?



  2. Which famous home appliances company started in 1901 with the introduction of its first product - Lux lamp, a kerosene lamp for outdoor use?



  3. What famous brand of home appliances is owned by Gillette, the world leader in shaving products?

II. BRAND PLAY

Talking of humour in advertising, John Caples, the famous copywriter once wrote: ``Avoid humour. You can entertain a million people and not sell one of them. There is not a single humorous line in two of the most influential books in the world - the Bible and the Sears catalog.'' Generally true, although I can't entirely agree with him on this count. Check out the following soap brands and match them with the companies that market them. (1 pt each) 

     1. `Dettol'                             a. Wipro
 2. `Camay'                              b. Hindustan Lever
 3. `Santoor'                            c. Godrej
 4. `Savlon'                             d. Procter & Gamble
 5. `Fairglow'                           e. Reckitt & Coleman
