I. HOWZATT!

Here are some questions on famous sporting goods brands. Are you game? (4 pts each)

  1. In which category are these brands famous: Gunn & Moore, Gray- Nicolls, SG, Slazenger.


  2. Which brand, with a bird as its logo, is the world's largest manufacturer of cricket balls and has also been the exclusive supplier of hockey balls for the Olympics since 1984?


  3. Which famous brand of sports goods was once endorsed by Bill Gates?


  4. Which famous apparel company owns the brand Prince, famous for its tennis racquet and Killer Loop sunglasses?


  5. Which famous manufacturer of sports goods started as a producer of wooden flats for fishing nets in 1947, and was founded by the Japanese Minoru Yoneyama?


II. BASELINE PLAY

William Bernbach once said: ``In communication, familiarity breeds apathy.'' It is important in marketing to ensure that your ad is never over-exposed. Check out the following cigarette baselines. (1 pt each) 

    1. `Live life kingsize'                         a. Mild Seven
2. `Discover a passion'                         b. India Kings
3. `The moment you've waited for'               c. Gold Flake
4. `It's honeydew smooth'                       d. Four Square Kings
5. `Rule your world'                            e. Classic
