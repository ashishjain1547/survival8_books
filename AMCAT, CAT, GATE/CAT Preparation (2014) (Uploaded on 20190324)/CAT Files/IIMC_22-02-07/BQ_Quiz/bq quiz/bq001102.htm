I. The Numbers Game

Check your knowledge on brands that use numbers in their names, trademark or ad baseline. (4 pts each)

  1. How is the Minnesota Mining & Manufacturing Company better known as?



  2. Which company, founded in 1979 by Bob Metcalfe the principal inventor of Ethernet, adopted a name that indicated its focus on communication products for consumer and commercial markets?



  3. Which brand of Scotch whisky derives its name from an old Scottish legend which says that when you sip a good Scotch whisky, you hear a piper play?



  4. Which television channel, better known in India for its cricket telecasts out of Australia, has recently launched operations in India with a time-sharing agreement with Doordarshan?



  5. Which brand would you associate with the advertising icon Fido Dido?
