I. SHE 'S THE BOSS

Check your knowledge on some of the most powerful women in business. (4 pts each)

  1. She owns the media company Harpo Entertainment and her TV shows run in over 100 countries. Name her.



  2. Which woman chief executive was ranked No.1 for the second year in a row in Fortune's `50 Most Powerful Women in US Business'?



  3. Marjorie Scardino was recently ranked amongst the world's most powerful women. She heads a publishing group which owns The Financial Times and The Economist magazine among other things. Name the media group.



  4. What's common to the following leading companies: eBay, Hewlett Packard, Xerox, Ogilvy & Mather, MTV?



  5. Andrea Jung is generally credited to have turned around this company, during her seven-year tenure with it. Name the company .
