Brand Quest      (November 23, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. MARKETING MOMENTS

Check your knowledge on some famous marketing moments in history. ( 4 pts each)

  1. This year saw the launch of Yahoo!, Amazon.com and the famous launch of the Orange mobile phone service in the UK. Which year was this?



  2. In which year was the famous airline British Airways privatised?



  3. In which year did Coca-Cola decide to change its formulation, only to return to the original formulation after a huge furore in the market?



  4. Calvin Klein created a first of sorts in the fragrance market with a unisex scent CKOne and Sony entered the computer games market with PlayStation in this year. Which year was this?



  5. Henry Ford revolutionised the car market with the first mass- produced car, the Ford Model T. In which year was it launched?
