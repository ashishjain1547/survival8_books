I. ADWATCH

Check your knowledge on some famous TV commercials of Hindustan Lever (HLL), one of India's largest advertisers. (4 pts each)

  1. What famous soap brand has traditionally been advertised, across the world as the 'Beauty bar of film stars'?



  2. With which famous brand of HLL would you associate the following catchphrases/ acronyms a. ZPTO b. KarKar?



  3. A recent campaign for this famous brand focuses on the theme, '___ badal gaya', bringing into the limelight the fact that the formulation of this century-old product has changed. Name the brand.



  4. In the TV commercial for which brand did the famous Tabla player Zakir Hussain declare that he would quit playing the tabla if someone could find a better product?



  5. For which HLL brands do the following famous personalities appear a. Govinda b. Rahul Dravid?
