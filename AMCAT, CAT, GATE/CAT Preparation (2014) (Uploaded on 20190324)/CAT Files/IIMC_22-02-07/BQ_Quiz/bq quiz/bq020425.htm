Brand Quest      (April 25, 2002)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. BOOKSHELF

Check your knowledge of some of the famous business books written over the years. (4 pts each)

  1. Name the famous businessman and Chief Exective who wrote the bestsellers What they don't teach you at Harvard Business School and What they didn't teach me at Yale Law School.



  2. Name the famous book authored by Jean Marie Dru, a top advertising executive from TBWA, subtitled 'Overturning Conventions and Shaking Up the Marketplace'?



  3. Which famous two-word phrase would you associate with researcher Daniel Goleman, who has authored many books and articles on the subject (it is also the title of his first famous book published in 1995)?



  4. What is the irreverent title of the recent business bestseller authored by two consultants from Gallup, Marcus Buckingham and Curt Cuffman ( the book is subtitled 'What the World's Greatest Managers Do Differently' )



  5. What new management technique, currenly in vogue, was first introduced by Robert Kaplan and David Norton through their article in Harvard Business Review and later a book by the same name published in 1996?



II. MATCH PLAY

Many pharmaceutical brands are not advertised aggressively, but are still popular with Indian consumers. Match the following famous pharmaceutical brands with the companies that market them. (1 pt each) 

    1. Vicks Action 500     a. Parke-Davis
2. Avil                 b. Johnson & Johnson
3. Band-Aid             c. Procter & Gamble
4. Crocin               d. Hoechst Marion Roussel
5. Benadryl             e. SmithKline Beecham



III. COPY TEST

Following are words picked out of the body copy of a print ad, voice-over of a TVC or simply a baseline. Identify the brand. (3 pts each)
  1. '_____ hain jahan, tandurusti hain wahan'



  2. 'Itne smooth jaise ...Saurav ka square cut'



  3. 'Milkerian, sweetarian, fisherian, juicerian....be a freshetarian'



  4. 'Dor nahin. Public ki keench rahe hain'



  5. 'Sirf _____ mein hain anokhi Golden Eye technology, jo aankhon ko de aaram. Kyonki aankein hain anmol"



IV. TAKE YOUR PICK

Select the right answer. (2 pts each)
  1. What famous invention, of the late 19th century is credited to John S. Pemberton?

    • The radio
    • Coca-Cola's formula
    • Chocolate
    • Ice-cream



  2. The editor of which famous business publication resigned from her position, admitting that she had got 'too close' to the subject of her piece - Jack Welch during an interview?

    • Fortune
    • BusinessWeek
    • Harvard Business Review
    • Forbes



  3. With which famous international express shipments company would you associate the 'Jumbo Box'?

    • Federal Express
    • UPS
    • DHL
    • TNT



  4. With the PSU disinvestment drive gathering momentum, which private sector company has recently taken a 26 per cent stake in Hindustan Zinc?

    • Sterlite Industries
    • Binani Zinc
    • IndoGulf Corporation
    • Bhushan Steel



  5. Which famous multinational company uses the advertising baseline, 'Where do you want to go today?'

    • British Airways
    • GreyHound
    • Microsoft
    • Yahoo!
