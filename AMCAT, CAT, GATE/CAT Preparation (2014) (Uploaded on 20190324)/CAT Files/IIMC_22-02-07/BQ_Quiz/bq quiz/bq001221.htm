Brand Quest      (December 21, 2000)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. Potpourri

A mixed bag of questions this week. (4 pts each)

  1. What's common to the following brands: Life, Stop, Kashish and Karot?



  2. Name the Dutch publisher which is currently in the process of a takeover/merger with AC Nielsen, and also owns a majority stake in Indian research firm ORG-MARG?



  3. In a case of a bizarre product portfolio under one brand name, this company famous for its electric switches and plug points, also has a toothpaste with the same brand name and is currently planning the launch of talcum powder and hair oil. Name the brand.



  4. What's common to the following: Raga, Nebula, Fasttrack?



  5. With what offering would you associate the UK-based company Camelot, a consortium of Cadbury Schweppes, Racal and the Post Office among others?



II. BRAND PLAY

Jerry Della Femina once remarked: ``There is a great deal of advertising that is much better than the product. When that happens, all that the good advertising will do is put you out of business faster.'' Product claims in many categories are excessive to the extent that credibility of communication is sometimes in question. Match the following sub-brands of tyres with the companies that own/markets them. (1 pt each) 

    1. Amazer                     a. CEAT
2. Legend                     b. JK Tyres
3. Endura                     c. Dunlop
4. Ultima                     d. MRF
5. Spectrawide                e. Apollo



III. COPY TEST

  3. `A touch of ___ adds comfort, fit and freedom of movement to great clothes, and something more to you'

  5. `Your next texture... Your next fabric'
