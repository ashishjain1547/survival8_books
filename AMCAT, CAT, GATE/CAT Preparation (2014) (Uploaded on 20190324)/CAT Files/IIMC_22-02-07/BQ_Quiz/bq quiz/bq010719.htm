Brand Quest      (July 19, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. LINES FROM TEXT BOOKS

Check your knowledge on some famous international advertising lines which have become textbook case studies: (4 points each)

  1. With which company would you identify the catchline, ``We're number two. We try harder''? Who is the number one in the case in question?



  2. Complete the last two words in the famous advertisement for Rolls Royce: `At sixty miles an hour the loudest noise in the new Rolls-Royce comes from the ______' ?



  3. With which product would you associate the campaign, `Where's your moustache'?



  4. With which international appliance brand would you associate the line, ``Our repairmen are the loneliest guys in town''?



  5. Which brand would you associate with the famous line, ``Come to where the flavour is''?

III. COPY TEST

Following are words picked out of the body copy of a print ad, voice-over of a TV commercial, or simply, a baseline for the brand. Can you identify the brand advertised? (3 points each)
  1. `Your childhood mate, your family friend, your business partner, your old age companion ... is there for you. Always.'



  2. `Grab a quick lunch in Italy and still make it to your dinner appointment in Thailand.....Business has changed. Shouldn't your hotel?'



  3. `Because life insurance is also about other people's lives.'



  4. `Talk to me.'



  5. `Get more from your money. Get more from life.'
