In Class V there are 102 girls and 100 boys.
In Class VI there are 103 girls and 104 boys.
In Class VII there are 107 girls and 107 boys.
In Class VIII there are 107 girls and 109 boys.
In Class IX there are 94 girls and 112 boys.
In Class X there are 96 girls and 115 boys.
In Class XI there are 93 girls and 97 boys.
In Class XII there are 107 girls and 99 boys.


Each student has to select only one extra curricular activity. The 
break up of students taking different activity is as follows :

In Class V, 23 students take Sports, 35 students take Music, 28 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class V take 
other hobby classes.

In Class VI, 26 students take Sports, 35 students take Music, 36 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class VI 
take other hobby classes.

In Class VII, 43 students take Sports, 28 students take Music, 32 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class VII 
take other hobby classes.

In Class VIII, 54 students take Sports, 59 students take Music, 51 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class VIII 
take other hobby classes.

In Class IX, 48 students take Sports, 60 students take Music, 55 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class IX 
take other hobby classes.

In Class X, 48 students take Sports, 54 students take Music, 56 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class X take 
other hobby classes. 

In Class XI, 52 students take Sports, 55 students take Music, 57 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class XI 
take other hobby classes.

In Class XII, 57 students take Sports, 54 students take Music, 56 
students take Art and craft and the remaining of the students in Class XII 
take other hobby classes.


MARK 1 if only statement A is correct.
MARK 2 if only Statement B is correct.
Mark 3 if both are correct.
Mark 4 if both statement is false.

B. The maximum difference between number of girls and boys is in class

Q2. A. The total number of girls is more than total number of boys.

number of students taking Music.

other activities.

class V who have not taken either Sports, music or Art & craft.

total number of students taking Music.

Art & craft.

Q5. A. 30 % of students of class XI have taken Art and craft.

Q6. A. There are 1652 students in total from Class V to class XII.

Q8. A. In total the difference between number of student taking Art & 
craft and sports is 20.
B. In total the difference between number of student taking Music and 
sports is 20.

Q9. A. 43 % of the total students have taken sports.
B. 23 % of the total students have taken Music.

Q10. A. Class VIII has the maximum number of students.
B. Class XI has the minimum number of students .

Q11. A. 27.5 % of students of Class V have taken sports.
B. More than 30 % of students of Class XII have taken sports.


Q12. A. The total number of students who have taken either sports , 
music or art & craft of Class VIII is more than the total number of 
students who have taken either sports , music or art & craft of Class IX.

other hobby classes (that is they have not taken either sports , music or

Q13. A. More than 33 % of students of class V have taken either sports 
or music.

music.

Q14. A. More than 50 % of the students of class XI have taken either 
sports or music.

or music.
