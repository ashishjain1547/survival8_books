II. BOOK PLAY

David Ogilvy once said, ``The customer is not a moron; she is your wife.'' A sound thought which should really drive loyalty relationships for brands since customers are really the strongest strategic assets for a company! Match the following famous books on relationship marketing with their famous authors. (1 pt each) 

    1.  Loyalty Effect                      a.  Stan Rapp & Tom Collins
2.  One-to-One Future                   b.  Lester Wunderman
3.  Maximarketing                       c.  Frederick Reichheld
4.  Being Direct                        d.  Drayton Bird
5.  Commonsense Direct Marketing        e.  Don Peppers & Martha Rogers
