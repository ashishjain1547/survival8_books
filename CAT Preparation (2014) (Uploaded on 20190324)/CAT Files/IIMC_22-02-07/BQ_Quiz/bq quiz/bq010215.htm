Brand Quest      (February 15, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. Indian Terrain

Check your knowledge of some new Indian companies that have been in the news recently. (4 pts each)

  1. Which famous software company was founded, among others, by former senior executives from Wipro and whose logo has been designed by a handicapped student of the Spastics Society of Karnataka?



  2. Formed by a breakaway group of executives who were in the Tanishq division of Titan, this company is planning to launch branded jewellery nationwide through a chain of exclusive stores. Name the company.



  3. Name the company which has successfully launched three apparel brands - Indigo Nation, Scullers and Ironwood - in less than two years after its launch in the country.



  4. Which is the famous brand of shirts launched by Provogue, which has gained prominence within a year of its launch?



  5. One of the most successful Indian TV content production houses, it extended its business to broadcast by setting up its own TV channel in April 2000. Name the company and the channel it established?



II. BOOK PLAY

Walt Disney once wrote: ``There is more treasure in books than in all the pirates' loot on Treasure Island... and best of all, you can enjoy these riches every day of your life.'' There is a plethora of books by corporate chieftains on business and their own success stories. Check out the following famous books by CEOs and match them with their authors. (1 pt each) 

    1. Only the Paranoid Survive           a. Sam Walton
2. The Road Ahead                      b. John Sculley
3. Made in America                     c. Bill Gates
4. Made in Japan                       d. Andy Grove
5. Odyssey - Pepsi to Apple            e. Akio Morita
