I. Management Tomes

See how much you know about some famous business books written recently. (4 pts each)

  1. Name the author of the bookThe End of Marketing as we Know it. He was earlier the Chief Marketing Officer of Coca-Cola, and hit the headlines for spearheading the disastrous launch of New Coke in the mid-Eighties .



  2. One of the pathbreaking works about the Internet, this book was authored by Mary Modahl, head of research at Forrester Research Inc. Name the book.



  3. Adrian Slywotzky is well known for his best selling management books such as Value Migration, Profit Zone and Profit Patterns. His books were co-written with other consultants at the management consultancy he represented. Name the firm.



  4. One of the most prolific writers on branding in the last decade, his three famous works Managing Brand Equity, Building Strong Brands and Brand Leadership are acknowledged tomes on branding. Name him.



  5. What is the title of the famous book by Seth Godin, head of direct marketing at Yahoo! that outlines his new approach to the business of marketing communications?



II. BRAND PLAY

2. Bagpiper               b. IDV
