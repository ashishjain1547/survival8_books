I. Pot Pourri

A mixed bag of questions this week. (4 pts each)

  1. Jack Welch, perhaps the world's most highly regarded CEO, has postponed his retirement to see through GE's takeover of which company?



  2. What controversial ad featuring a policewoman kissing a convict has been banned from release on Doordarshan after censure by the I&B ministry?



  3. What's common to these following senior banking professionals: Rana Talwar of Standard Chartered Bank, Aman Mehta of HSBC, Aditya Puri of HDFC Bank and Deepak Satwalekar of HDFC Insurance



  4. Under what brand name has the Tata group recently launched its Internet service (under the company banner of Tata Internet)?



  5. The recent hit Hollywood movie Cast Awayis based on a man(played by Tom Hanks) marooned on a Pacific island for more than four years. In which company does the man work - a company central to the theme of the entire movie?



II. BOOK PLAY

David Ogilvy once said: ``Set exorbitant standards and give your people hell when they don't live up to them. There is nothing so demoralising as a boss who tolerates second-rate work.'' It is standards such as these that have built venerable corporations, which have later become the subjects of many books. Check out the following famous books on some large corporations, and match them with the company on which they are based (1 pt each) 

    1. `For God, Country and _____'                a. Microsoft
2. `Leading by Design'                         b. Coca-Cola
3. `On a Clear Day you can see ______'         c. IKEA
4. `Well made in America'                      d. General Motors
5. `Inside Out'                                e. Harley Davidson
