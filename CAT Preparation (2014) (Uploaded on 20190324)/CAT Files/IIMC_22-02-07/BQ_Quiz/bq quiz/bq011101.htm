I. GREAT BRANDS

Check your knowledge on some famous brands. They have one thing in common, they all start with the same letter. (4 pts each)

  1. Which famous company, now owned by Ford, was started by the pioneer Walter Jacobs who rented out Ford Model T cars in 1918?



  2. This premium retail store has only one major outlet in the world at Knightsbridge in London. The Chairman of this store is Mohammed Al Fayed. Name the store.



  3. This American ice-cream brand started by Reuben Mattus in the '60s, has a Danish brand name. Name the brand.



  4. What products would you identify with these international brands:(a) Hovis (b) Hoover (c) Hanes



  5. Which famous brand owned by Wolverine Worldwide uses a little dog, a soft-eyed beagle, as its symbol?
