I. BUSINESS TERMS

Check your knowledge on some of the words & phrases from the world of business. (4 pts each)

  1. In relation to cubicles & office layouts, what is 'hot-desking' ?



  2. In the study of periodic business perfomanace or sales analysis, what is the 'hockey stick' ?



  3. Where would you encounter the words, 'Sdn Bhd' and what does it mean ?
