I. Little Bites

How good is your knowledge of biscuit brands? Crunch on these questions. (4 pts each)

  1. What much-advertised brand of biscuits is owned by Surya Foods & Agro Pvt Ltd? And who is its famous endorser?


  2. Which company is the world's largest manufacturer of biscuits and cookies?


  3. United Biscuits, a large biscuit maker in Europe, has launched a brand of biscuits in India. Name it?


  4. Which company manufactures the Marie biscuits which, according to their TVC are `Very English, Very Marie'?


  5. Parle Products recently released a print campaign that obviously referred to Britannia without naming it, claiming the later copied Parle's biscuits. Which Britannia brands match the following Parle biscuits: (a) Monaco (b) Krackjack (c) Parle - G



II. Baseline Play

Raymond Rubicam once wrote, ``Advertising has a responsibility to behave properly. I proved that you can sell products without bamboozling the public.'' Unfortunately, this can't be the claim of every advertising man. Ads frequently resort to the mundane or to the other extreme, bombast, quite often with disastrous results. Check out how some of the car brands perform on this count. (1 pt each) 

    
1. `The complete family car'                    a. Maruti Baleno
2. `The josh machine'                           b. Maruti Esteem
3. `Pure driving luxury'                        c. Maruti Wagon R
4. `Sleek. Silent. Spirited'                    d. Hyundai Santro
5. `Where ideas take the front seat'            e. Ford Ikon
