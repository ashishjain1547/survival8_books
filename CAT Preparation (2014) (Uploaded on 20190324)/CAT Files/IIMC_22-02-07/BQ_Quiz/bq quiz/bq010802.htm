Brand Quest      (August 02, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. Most Valuable Brands

International business magazine BusinessWeek recently carried a cover issue on the world's most valuable brands, based on research study by Interbrand. Check out if you are in line with their findings. (4 pts each)

  1. There is only one European brand in the top 10 valuable brands in the world, the rest of the positions being occupied by US brands. Name the European brand.



  2. There are no brands from Britain in the top 50 most valuable brands. The highest ranked brand from Britain is at 52. Name the brand.



  3. Three brands in the top 100 most valuable brands are from France, predictably from the fashion and food industry. Name the three brands.



  4. Which banking organisation ranks the highest in its category and ranks overall 13th in the list of world's most valuable brands?



  5. Which brand is ranked as the world's most valuable brand ?



II. Baseline Play

Check out the following interior decoration brands - and match them with their baselines. (1 pt each) 

    1.  `Create a world of your own'                a.  Eurocon Tiles
2.  `24 Carat Living'                           b.  Nitco Tiles
3.  `Tiles with a little imagination'           c.  Johnson Tiles
4.  `The only tiles with no size variation'     d.  Somany Tiles
5.  `Not just tiles, Lifestyles'                e.  GoldCoin Tiles



III. COPY TEST

Following are words picked out of the body copy of a print ad, voice-over of a TV commercial, or simply, a baseline for the brand. Can you identify the brand advertised? (3 pts each)
  1. `_____ in my hair. Mischief in the air'



  2. `Just lagao. Kuch bhi khao'



  3. `___ Matrix. The best flat TV. If you're still asking questions, you obviously haven't seen it.'



  4. `If it's in your heart, it's on the cards'



  5. `You don't leave soap on your skin. Why leave it in your clothes?'
