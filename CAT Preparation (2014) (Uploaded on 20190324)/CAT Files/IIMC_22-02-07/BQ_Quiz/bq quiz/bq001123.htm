I. Agency FAQs

Discover how ad-savvy you are by answering these questions on Indian ad agencies.(4 pts each)

  1. Which ad agency was founded by the famous writer and advertising personality, Frank Simoes, in 1967, although it was later taken over by a different group in the early 1990s?



  2. She was a creative hotshot with Contract and Enterprise, and came back to advertising after a long break with her own eponymously named creative agency. Name the agency and the lady in question.



  3. Which famous Indian advertising agency was founded by Arun Nanda, Ajit Balakrishnan and Mohammed Khan?



  4. Name the foreign partners in the following Indian ad agencies: a. Ulka b. Anthem



  5. In order to increase business, there was a trend among ad agencies a few years ago to start a second agency to take on competing accounts. With which company are the following agencies aligned: a. Contract b. Karishma (now defunct) c. SSC & B



II. BRAND PLAY

Mark Twain, once said: ``Many a small thing has been made large by the right kind of advertising.'' There is a great deal of advertising in categories such as stationery which were really not advertised in the mass media in India until a decade ago. Match the following stationery brands with the company that markets them in India. (1 pt each) 

    1. Reynolds                     a. Camlin
2. Natraj                       b. GM Pens
3. Camel                        c. Pidilite
4. Acron                        d. Luxor
5. Paper Mate                   e. Hindustan Pencils
