I. Role Models

Have you heard of some of these famous models and tools of marketing and corporate strategy? (4 pts each)

  1. With which famous ad agency would you associate the Brand Asset Valuator (BAV) which measures a brand on four parameters: differentiation, relevance, esteem and knowledge?


  2. With which management consultancy would you associate the 7-S strategy framework consisting of Structure, Strategy, Systems, Shared Values, Skills, Style and Staff?


  3. The Boston Consulting Group introduced the growth-share matrix in the early 1970s. What is the common terminology for the four quadrants of the famous BCG matrix?


  4. One advertising model for assessing the role of feelings is the FCB Grid developed by ad agency Foote Cone Belding. What are the coordinates of the matrix?


  5. What kind of plan would you associate with ad agency J. Walter Thompson or its Indian arm HTA?



II. Baseline Play

Jeff Bezos of Amazon.com once remarked: ``I wake up every morning terrified, not of the competition but of our customers.'' The Net bug has really caught on, with heavy ad outlays to attract potential online customers. Check out how memorable the baselines of these Web sites are. (1 pt each) 

    1. `India's most trusted website'                 a. Rediff.com

5. `The difference between .com and .commonsense' e.Indiacar.com
