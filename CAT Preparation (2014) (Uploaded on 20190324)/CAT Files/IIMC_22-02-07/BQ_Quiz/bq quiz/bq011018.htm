Brand Quest      (October 18, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. PotPourri

A mixed bag of questions this week. (4 pts each)

  1. Which brand is slated to be the first to use the father and son duo of Amitabh and Abhishek Bachchan as celebrity endorsers?



  2. Which Indian movie was recently in the news for its inclusion as a case study in leadership skills by IIM Indore?



  3. One of the world's most famous ad directors, he was born and brought up in India. He is famous for his Smirnoff and Nike commercials and recently directed his first movie The Cell. Name him.



  4. Which famous TV commercial in the US took a pot-shot at exorbitant advertising rates with a commercial (aired during SuperBowl 2000) that ended with the lines ,``Well, we just wasted $2,000,000. What are you doing with your money?''



  5. Which TV channel has been in the news for its coverage of the Bin Laden crisis, especially for having carried all broadcasts of Osama Bin Laden to audiences in West Asia?
