I. Birds of a feather

Soar with these brands linked to the names of birds. (4 pts each)

  1. What famous brand of whisky founded by Mattew Gloag in the late 19th century derives its name from a Scottish bird sketched by the founder's daughter?



  2. What brand name, also the name of a bird, is common to a leading retailer in England and a famous beer in India?



  3. Swarovski has been a leading brand in cutglass crystal for over a century. What bird has it adopted as its logo since 1988 to reflect its purity and elegance?



  4. This bird is found in only one island country though its brand variant is found in little tins all over the world. The name of the bird was adopted by the company's founder William Ramsay out of affection for his wife who hailed from this country. Name the brand.



  5. Identify the following brands which have their names based on names of birds: a) Fabric whitener b) Book publisher
