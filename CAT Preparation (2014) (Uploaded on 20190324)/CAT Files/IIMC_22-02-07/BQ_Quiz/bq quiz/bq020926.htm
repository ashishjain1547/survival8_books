Brand Quest      (September 26, 2002)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. BRAND PERSONALITY

Check your knowledge on the world of celebrity endorsements. (4 pts each)

  1. Which famous personality is the common link between the following brands - Pantaloon, AMP Sanmar and MRF?



  2. Crowning Glory, the soap brand owned by Godrej, is closely associated with its famous endorser. Name the celebrity who endorses it.



  3. Which international brand faced considerable public embarrassment a few years ago when its star endorser and US sportsman O.J. Simpson was embroiled in a murder accusation?



  4. International celebrities in Indian ads is not a new phenomenon. For which suitings brand did cricketer Geoffrey Boycott wield the willow to give tennis stars Leander Paes and Mahesh Bhupati a few lessons in cricket?



  5. The cricketers Vivian Richards and Allan Border endorsed an Indian brand more than a decade ago. Name the brand.
