II. BRAND PLAY

On the increasing trend of sales promotions, as far back as two decades ago, David Ogilvy wrote: ``The manufacturer who finds himself up the creek is the short-sighted opportunist who siphons off all his advertising dollars for short-term promotions.'' This is one trend which shampoo brands have kept away from in the recent past, with most of them heavily spending on brand- building. Check out the following famous shampoo brands and match them with the companies that market them. (1 pt each) 

    1. `Halo'                               a. Johnson & Johnson
2. `Sunsilk'                            b. Colgate Palmolive 
3. `Ultra Doux'                         c. Hindustan Lever
4. `Nizral Blue'                        d. Revlon
5. `Flex'                               e. Laboratoires Garnier
