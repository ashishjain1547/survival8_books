I. MASTERPIECES

Check your knowledge on some famous international advertisements of the past half-century. (4 pts each)

  1. This stark, controversial ad showed a little girl picking petals off a daisy, and ended by showing the mushroom cloud of an atomic explosion. What was this ad (a successful campaign in the 1960s)?



  2. This TV commercial titled 'Revolution' had no voiceovers (and no supers) and used the famous Beatles song, (the first-time ever that a Beatles song featured in a TV Commercial) of the same name. Name the brand featured in this ad that brought wor ldwide fame for the little-known advertising agency Wieden & Kennedy.



  3. An expensive TV commercial, this famous ad called 'Happy Face' choerographed 4,000 people, wearing red, white and blue caps, to form a giant living face shot from overhead by an airborne camera. Name the brand it was for.



  4. Which famous US brand of moccasins gained fame with audacious advertising in the late 1980s with lines such as 'We stole their land, their buffalo and their women. Then we went back for their shoes'?



  5. Which famous US magazine launched the 'Perception vs Reality' campaign successfully in the 1980s and sustained it - to change the perception of advertisers that its readers weren't pot-smoking hippies, but individuals with high purchasing power (subsequently one of the most imitated themes in world advertising)?
