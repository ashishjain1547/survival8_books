I. POTPOURRI

A mixed bag of questions this week. (4 pts each)

  1. Which famous CEO, currently applauded for turning around the fortunes of a famous computer company, reportedly takes home a salary of $1 per year?



  2. What's common to Code Red, Melissa and Anna Kournikova?



  3. What was unique about the purchase of a Gulfstream V business jet by American Internet tycoon Mark Cuban in 1999 for $40 million?



  4. Dave Thomas has appeared in 652 TV commercials since April 1989. The unique aspect is that he was the founder of the company - a famous brand of fast food restaurants. Name the brand.



  5. Name the world's largest television network that is beamed to over 210 countries?

  4. 'Because what I breathe is what we breathe'
