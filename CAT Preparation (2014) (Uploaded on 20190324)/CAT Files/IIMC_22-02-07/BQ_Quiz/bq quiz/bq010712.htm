I. THE END GAME

Check your knowledge on the products and brands related to the alphabet Z ( 4 pts each):

  1. What brand of cigarette lighter invented by George Blaisdell is known for its famous lifetime guarantee against mechanical defects?



  2. With what product of everyday use is the company YKK synonymous with?



  3. It is considered one of the most successful pharmaceutical brand. Name this famous ulcer medication tablet of Glaxo?



  4. It is a famous German brand, which is synonymous with binoculars and is known to have been the pet accompaniment of famous men like Ernest Hemingway and Albert Einstein. Name the brand.



  5. What brand of computers in India is also a famous yesteryear brand of television, which was popular in the early part of the 20th century in the US?
