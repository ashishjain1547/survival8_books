II. BRAND PLAY

Winston Churchill once said, ``Advertising nourishes the consuming power of men. It sets up before a man the goal of a better home, better clothing, better food for himself and his family. It spurs individual exertion and greater production.'' The magnitude of advertising spend on flat televisions in the country, a category out of reach of almost all Indian households, has always intrigued me but then maybe it makes the brand aspirational even if that product doesn't sell !!
