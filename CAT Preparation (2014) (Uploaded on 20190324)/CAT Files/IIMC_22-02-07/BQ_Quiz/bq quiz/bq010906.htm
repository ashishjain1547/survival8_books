Brand Quest      (September 06, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. BRANDSTAND

Check your knowledge on some famous international brands. (4 pts each)

  1. Synonymous with synthetic grass, this brand was originally created in the 1960s in response to a requirement from the Ford Foundation for an urban playing surface to improve children's fitness. Name this brand, currently owned by Southwest Recreational Industries.



  2. Which brand, launched in 1929 by the London Rubber Company, originally derived its name from its qualities of `durability, reliability and excellence'?



  3. What was the famous result of an argument in 1951, over which the fastest game bird was, during a shooting party in Ireland led by Sir Hugh Beaver?



  4. What was started in 1973 by passionate travellers Tony and Maureen Wheeler, with a brand name drawn from the movie Mad Dogs and Englishman, which also is the name of a song by Joe Cocker?



  5. Which was the first brand to launch the Swiss Army knife in 1891 and is officially allowed the use of the line `The Original Swiss Army Knife' in all its communication?
