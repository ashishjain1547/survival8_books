I. ONE FOR THE ROAD

Some of the oldest, and still growing, brands in the world are premium liqueurs. How up-to-date are you about them? (4 pts each)

  1. Made from aged Scotch, mixed with heather honey and herbs, its origins date back more than 250 years when, legend says, a prince concocted it. The recipe was a secret till it was mass produced from 1911. Name the brand.


  2. Which company markets Godiva chocolate liqueur, an extension of the famous chocolate brand?


  3. Created in 1859, this eponymously named liqueur brand was perhaps the first crystal clear product in the category and is made from a mixture of bitter and sweet orange peels. Name it.


  4. Which company markets Kahlua, one of the world's largest liqueur brands, and also owns, among others, the Baskin Robbins ice-cream chain and the Dunkin' Donuts chain?


  5. One of the world's largest selling liqueurs, it was also one of the most recently launched brands (in 1974) and is made from a mixture of milk and whisky, generally considered two unmixable ingredients?
