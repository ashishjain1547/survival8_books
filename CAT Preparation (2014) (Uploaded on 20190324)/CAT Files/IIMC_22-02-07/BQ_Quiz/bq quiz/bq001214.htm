I. In the News

Check out how much you know about recent happenings in the world of international business. (4 pts each)

  1. Why was US executive Jeffrey Immelt prominently in the business news?



  2. Name the famous French media conglomerate that took over the Seagram Co and is currently looking for buyers for its liquor division?



  3. In a major cross-country Internet takeover, it was announced that France's largest Internet service provider (ISP) will takeover FreeServe, UK's largest ISP. Name the French ISP.

II. BRAND PLAY

Jeff Richards, an advertising academic once wrote: ``Advertising practitioners are interpreters. But unlike foreign language interpreters, ad people must constantly learn new languages. They must understand the language of each new product and speak the language of each new target audience.'' In the new Internet world, have advertising agencies learnt to talk to consumers in their language? Match the following Internet service provider brands with the companies that market them. (1 pt each) 

    1. Mantra Online      a. Sterling Infotech
2. Powersurfer        b. Bharti
3. TringTring         c. BSES
4. NetKracker         d. Aptech
5. Dishnet DSL        e. Wipro



III. COPY TEST

  2. `Style speaks a single language. Bond with the best'



  3. `Until I came here I thought words beginning with `un' referred to things negative'
