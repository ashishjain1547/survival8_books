Brand Quest      (October 25, 2001)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. GREAT ADS

Check your knowledge on some famous brands and the great international advertising behind them. (4 pts each)

  1. For over 20 years, the ad for this brand has been carried only in glossy magazines and only in two-word copy format with the first word being the brandname itself. Name this brand of Swedish origin.



  2. With which famous TV commercial would you associate a famous English novel by George Orwell?



  3. It was the brand that made David Ogilvy famous. Which shirt brand's ads had the famous man with an eye patch in the 1950s, which catapulted David Ogilvy to fame?



  4. Breaking away from tradition, this women's perfume brand had a man's name. The ad created a furore in the late '60s featuring a lady patting a man's backside. Name the brand.



  5. Till the early '50s, Philip Morris had a cigarette brand for women that didn't sell. Taking a risk, the brand was repositioned as a rugged man's cigarette, under the same brandname and the rest became history. Name the brand.
