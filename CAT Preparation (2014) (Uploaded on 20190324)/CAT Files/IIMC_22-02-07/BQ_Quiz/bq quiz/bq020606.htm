Brand Quest      (June 06, 2002)

To get answer, point at the ANSWER icon

I. NAMES

Check your knowledge of some famous brand names. (4 pts each)

  1. What irreverent brand of T-shirts, with its origins in the UK, derives its brandname from a rearrangement of the letters of a 'four letter word'?



  2. What brand of pressure cookers is almost generic to the category in the UK, the brand's country of origin? (It is also famous as the company David Ogilvy worked for in the UK before moving into the ad business in the US)



  3. What word, commonly used for unsolicited mail, was originally the brandname of luncheon meat?



  4. What Japanese sports brand is famous for its equipment used in badminton, tennis and golf?



  5. One of the oldest German industrial establishments, it made the first pencils as early as 1761. Name this famous stationery brand.

III. COPY TEST

Following are words picked out of the body copy of a print ad, voice-over of a TVC or simply a baseline. Identify the brand. (3 pts each)
  1. 'Amazing how much warmth the world's most powerful air-conditioner generates'



  2. 'Voice. Video and Data. If a human being fails to carry even one, society calls him handicapped. What does it call your network?



  3. '20 years ago we invented personal computing. Now, we are reinventing it.



  4. 'They may not make it to the 2006 World Cup. But ____'s 5-year warranty will ensure you do'



  5. 'Talk in colour. Presenting the world's first TFT-LCD colour phone'
