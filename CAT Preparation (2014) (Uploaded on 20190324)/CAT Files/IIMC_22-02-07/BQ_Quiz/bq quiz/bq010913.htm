I. THE MONEY MANAGERS

Check your knowledge on some famous organisations in the world of finance. (4 pts each)

  1. What is the name of the financial company, one of the most successful investment companies in the world, which is associated with the world's most famous investor -- Warren Buffett?



  2. It was a financial advisor to national governments in the late 19th century and was instrumental in the initial structuring and financing of giant corporations such as AT & T, US Steel, General Electric among others. Name this organisation.



  3. The US banking act of 1933 (the Glass-Steagall Act) resulted in the split of JP Morgan into two organisations - focussed on commercial and investment banking. Name the investment bank which was created following the division .



  4. Which company pioneered the concept of discount brokerage in the late 1970s, and also led the online investment revolution in the 1990s?



  5. Which company is famous for its 1971 commercial `____ is bullish on America' and is known for its famous `bull' logo?
