I. THE EASTERN SUN

Check your knowledge of some famous Korean companies.(4 pts each)

  1. LG Electronics adopted its name on Jan 1, 1995. How was it known earlier and what does `LG' stand for?



  2. The Japanese conglomerates are called the `Sogososhas'.What are the Korean conglomerates called?



  3. Korean businessmen are not generally known for their books unlike their American counterparts. But which Korean businessman has written the best-seller *Every Street is paved with Gold?



  4. Korea originally had three major car producers: two of them being Daewoo and Hyundai. Name the third company which was taken over by Hyundai in 1998?



  5. Which company has been associated with sponsoring different events from the Olympics to the Nobel prize. Its chairman, Kun Hee Lee was also a member of the International Olympic Committee?

  5. `When you realise it was discovered 1000 metres beneath the earth's surface, one thing becomes clear: Mother Nature is a woman'
