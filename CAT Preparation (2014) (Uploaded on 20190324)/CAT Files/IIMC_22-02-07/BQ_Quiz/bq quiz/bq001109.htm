I. Cars

  2. Which brand of cars had a double red `R' monogram, adopted in 1905, and later changed to black in 1930?



  3. The monogram of a leading international car brand was inspired by a propeller. Name the brand.



  4. A complex monogram, in the form of a shield with the left part representing a cross and the right part with a symbol of a snake, it represents the arms of the city of Milan. Name the sports car brand that sports this logo.

II. BRAND PLAY

John Wanamaker, many decades ago wrote: ``In writing advertising it must always be kept in mind that the customer often knows more about the goods than the advertising writers because they have had experience in buying them, and any seeming deception in a statement is costly, not only in the expense of the advertising, but in the detrimental effect produced upon the customer, who believes she has been misled.'' Match the following brands of soap that claim to use natural ingredients with the companies that market them in India. (1 pt each) 

    1. Moti                         a. Wipro
2. Nikhar                       b. Dorcas Distributors
3. Medimix                      c. Godrej
4. Margo                        d. Hindustan Lever
5. Shikakai                     e. Calcutta Chemicals
