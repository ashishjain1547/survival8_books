I. NAME'S THE GAME

Check your knowledge on famous brandnames and their origins. (4 pts each)

  1. In 1958, a Frenchman launched the first low-cost, disposable ballpoint pens in Europe, which revolutionised the writing instrument market. The brandname was a derived from his own name. Name the brand.



  2. Name the famous African gazelle whose name was adopted as the brandname of athletic footwear by grandchildren of the original founder of the company Joseph Foster, before they dramatically grew the business.



  3. Which of the following brands does not derive its name from its founder: Maggi, Kraft, Jeep, Braun, Bacardi



  4. Japanese Businessman Shojiro Ishibashi founded this tyre company is 1931 - and adopted as its brandname an English translation of his own surname with an eye on the foreign markets. Name this famous brand of tyres, which you seldom associate with its Japanese origins.



  5. Domex is the Hindustan Lever brand famous in India for its plank 'Kills all known germs'. What is the name of the original British brand on which the product is based (including the tagline), and takes its name from ?
