I. They Invented It

Check your knowledge on some famous inventions that have impacted business over the years. (4 pts each)

  1. With which invention made in 1851 and relating to processed food would you associate the American Gail Borden?



  2. In 1851, he pioneered commercial news transmission, particularly on the trans-Atlantic route, through a combination of carrier pigeons and the newly evolved telegraph medium. Name him.



  3. Who, in the early 1850s, invented the safety system for elevators which revolutionised the elevator market?



  4. Which famous American invented the bifocal lens in the late 1700s; an invention which revolutionised the optics market?



  5. Which famous pair of brothers were the first to fit a motor vehicle with tyres with inner tubes in 1885; a name today synonymous with automobile tyres?
