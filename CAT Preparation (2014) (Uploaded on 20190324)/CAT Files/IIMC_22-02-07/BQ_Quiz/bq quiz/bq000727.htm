II. BRAND PLAY

Jules Henry wrote in 1963 on the impact of advertising on children: ``If advertising has invaded the judgment of children, it has also forced its way into the family, an insolent usurper of parental function, degrading parents to mere intermediaries between their children and the market. This indeed is a social revolution in our time!'' Check out the following fruit beverage brands and match them with the companies that own/market them. (1 pt each) 

    1. `Life'         a. Coca-Cola
2. `Noga'         b. Parle Agro
3. `Real'         c. Enkay Texofoods
4. `Frooti'       d. Dabur foods
5. `Maaza'        e. Maharashtra Agro
