Hi puys,

Congrats to all those who cracked it

My profile :
Graduation: B.Tech. ECE engg. from Jaypee University of Information Technology, Solan (H.P.)
XII: 68% (state board, Jharkhand)
X: 76% (state board Bihar)

WorkEx: 5 months in IT

Excurrics : Dramatics, writing, debating, poetry, admads etc

CAT details :
Score: 99.83 pc (DI: 99.43, VA: 95.91, QA: 99.47 )
Calls: A,C, I, K (Did not apply for any Non-IIM college)
Converted: C,I,K



About GD :

Though I talk a lot, I curbed my instincts slightly during all my GDs. The thing that perhaps clicked for me was my attentive listening (which I took care of reflecting, thru my body language too) & the ability to speak only at the right moment, a gentleman's approach I call it. I rarely interrupted anyone & even if it meant that my airtime was always below average, in hindsight, I was able to dispel any aura of negativity about me.


About PI :

All my PI's were good/above average, my deep interest in literature helped me a lot.


Of course, standard disclaimers apply i.e. this set of experience is my personal experience only, it may or may not click for any fellow puy.

Heading to Joka

Thanks a lot pg & buddy puys, your support played a very crucial role in my success.

Hello puys!
my journey for CAT began sometime in 2006, reached a tragic low on 19th november 2006( the D-DAY) , and led me into the precincts of PAGALGUY , looking primarily for some good preparatory material for CAT 07, and also for some motivation .

soon i realised that PG was a strong addiction, and i could not imagine a day without PG. though, i must admit, i have been passively involved with the site so far.

then came CAT 07, followed by an astounding result , some anxious weeks of GD/PI preparations , some more anxious weeks in waiting for the result and finally the results.

PG was there with me, through all of this.

and thanks to all this, i now get the honour of posting on this hallowed thread on PG.

as for the stats, here goes:
calls: A,C,L,I,K
converts:L,K
waitlists: I
joining: L

i wont comment on the interview experiences, nor will I venture to give any tips to the future aspirants. frankly speaking, even as of now, i am not able to make out the head or tails of what they look for in an interview.

all i can say is that, converts or no converts, the experience of an IIM interview is unforgettable.

I never thought I would get the opprtunity to post in this thread but here i am today. I feel i am one of luckiest people to have made it to the iims. From getting a meagre 23 in Verbal and a small total of 105 after CAT, I finally landed up with 120 marks total( don't know how my answers in verbal matched with that of the iims)- Ended up with a modest 99.40 and clearing cutoffs for all IIMs. After 3 unsuccessful attempts, I finally reached here in my fourth attempt

Here's My profile and calls:

Acads:

Xth: 87.8 CBSE
XIIth: 93.2 CBSE
Grad: 7.62/10
College, Year: IIT Kharagpur, 2005
Discipline: Aerospace Engineering

Extra Currics:

Above Average: Dramatics, Swimming, Dumb charades, Some Community Service as well.

CAT Percentiles:

QA: 96.76
DI: 96.27
VA: 98.93

Overall: 99.40

Work Ex:

Sales and Trading [Finance] -> 16 months
Structured Finance in a Bulge Bracket Investment Bank [I-Banking]- 19 months

Total work ex: 35 months (My profile is pretty unique and Strong for an MBA)

Calls: BLACKI

Other Calls through CAT: MDI, SPJain, IMT, IMI, KJSomaiya, Great Lakes

Calls through GMAT: NUS, ISB

Calls through JMET: IITB,IITD,IITKGP,IITM

Calls through NMAT: NMIMS

Calls through XAT: XIMB

Attended: Every College that I got a call from.

Converts: C[PGDCM], L, I, K

Other converts: SCMHRD, Great Lakes, IMI(PGDM), NUS, IMT-G [PGDM FT], IITB SJMSOM, IITKGP VGSOM, SPJain PGDM [Finance], KJ Somaiya, MDI PGPM,

WL: 60 at IIMA, 10 at XIMB

Rejects: B

Joining: C (Still decising between NUS and C)

A:

Essay: Average to Good
PI: Horrible, They literally told to spend another 4 years in engineering for getting my engg funda straight
Verdict: WL 60
Did I expect this? : No. I was expecting a reject
What would have attracted them: My Essay and my profile

GD+Essays: Very Good, My best GD Ever and good essays
PI: Rocking PI, Spoke very well- My best interview as well.

C:

GD: Average
PI: Below Average
Verdict: Selected for PGDCM
Did I expect this?: No. I was expecting a convert in PGDM as well.
What would have bugged them: Gave a very casual answer for Why MBA and gave a stupid answer for a simple maths problem.

L:

GD: Crappy topic. All spoke non sense
Essay: Pathetic Essay
PI: Below Average
Verdict: Selected
Did I expect this? : Not initially. I was initially expecting a reject. But then my percentils were high and had a good profile so thought that i should get through.


I:

GD: Business case study. Cracked it majorly.
PI: Very Good
Did I expect this: Yes

K:

GD: Fishmarket. One guy was hell bent on fighting with me and didn't let me speak.
PI: Average and Arbit
Verdict: Selected
Did I expect this: No. Because , the profs asked me things like which crackers i like in diwali. So thought that probably they have made up their mind seeing my poor performance in GD.
What went or me: Profile and percentiles.
