Well puys, this is an emotional moment for me.. .. So thought of giving you all a detailed account of my CAT journey....T'was my third attempt after all....

1st Attempt - CAT 03

CAT %ile - 96.5 .... ...Was supposed to be best prepared for this one....

Calls - Nil (as expected) .....B probably dint have crazy short-listing criteria back then.. ...No offence meant....

2nd Attempt - CAT 05

CAT %ile - 99.06

VA - 99.xx
QA - 97.xx
DI - 83.xx ... ...Screwed up big time....

Calls - A lone K call
Rejects - A, B, C, L, I, MDI, NITIE, SP Jain....(Expecting to get a call from MDI, NITIE, SP Jain et all with a measly 83 in DI, huh...??... ...)

K GD/PI melodrama - (6th Feb, 06)

My lone call and I reached late for the intv process.... ....Ran up the stairs of the CPP block (IIM B)...just to see the rest of the candidates walk in for the GD.....moi was held back.....Dreams shattered.... ....(10:40 a.m.)

However, the panel was kind enuf to consider me in the afternoon slot....

time - 2:15 p.m.

GD/PI started

GD - Had a satisfactory GD....3-4 entries...made a couple of valid points...was well accepted by the group.....

PI - Gang-R***d .... How can u expect to be late for an IIM interview and get away with it....???... Was the last one to be interviewed....the interview process culminated at 6:45 p.m... ....Was exhausted, worn-out and broken.... ....Had not had a grain of food thus far....dint feel like either.....

Converts - Nil (dint expect either)

3rd Attempt - CAT 06

CAT %ile - 99.13

VA - 98.98%ile
QA - 96.96 %ile
DI - 94.94 %ile (Dint get A because of this.... ...)

Calls - I, K, MDI, NITIE

Rejects A (Criteria not met) , B (Criteria..??...He he he..), C (Was DI probably), L (Still can't figure out), SP Jain.... ....(Now this was tooo much...99.xx with decent acads and 33 months work-exp wasn't gud enuf for them.... ...)

Converts - I , K , MDI, NITIE.....Phinally got to use the smiley

Hi All,

My profile :

X : 92 (ICSE)
XII : 73.7 (State Board)
Engg : 81 ( NIT Jamshedpur )
Work-ex : 33 months in an IT firm.

CAT : 99.60
Calls : BLACKI
Converts : BLACKI

GD/PI experience :

A : avg GD , horrible PI ( selected purely on profile , i guess . Had left the extra-currics column in my form absolutely blank )

B : avg GD, avg PI ( but got a feel after my interview that i would get thru, and did )

C : avg GD, avg PI ( PI went on for around 30-35 mins, they asked me all they could, and i answered almost everything. but the feeling after the interview was not good )

L : avg GD, avg PI ( they just discussed bihar ( my home state) throughout the interview, i accepted things were in a mess over there. nothing brilliant to talk about )

I : bad GD, horrible PI ( too much acads. but gave some good answers to a few general awareness qns. )

K : avg GD, avg PI ( PI started with the panel smiling and me confused, and eneded with me smiling and the panel totally at sea . )

My opinion :

1. your academic profile matters a hell lot, not as much in your rejection as in your selection. I mean, you can afford to make mistakes if your marks back you up .
2. for ppl with work-ex, acads do not matter, not even at IIMA
3. Please do not give any dumb answers in the PI. "Don't Know" is acceptable, "Bill Gates heads Apple" is not !
4. General Awareness questions matter much more than acads ones.
5. Beg Lady Luck to kiss you on both Cheeks, before a GD/PI.

All the Best,
Merv.

Joining A , BTW. and had not applied for any other insti apart from IIMS.
