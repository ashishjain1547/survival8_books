My results : repeating here - 16-06-2005, 08:47 PM
Posting my IIM results , an honour to be here on this thread
so here I go

X 79.6 U.P. board
XII 72.2 U.P. board
Appeared for IITJEE'99 and SCRA ( special class railway apprentice) which is a class I govt service .79 persons including me got the call.also cleared screening in JEE.
got kicked out in mains in JEE...was 1 of the 12 persons getting SCRA final call....decided against joining it amidst cries of me gone mad
Appeared for IITJEE next year too ...got 387 rank ...joined IIT Kanpur mechanical engg.
appeared for CAT 04 while in 4th year...got 96.7% ..no calls
appeared for CAT 05 after leaving my plum automobile industry job....99.45 %
calls : IIM A,C,L,I,K , NITIE ,MDI
converts : IIM C (PGDCM) ,L ,I ,NITIE, MDI
joining : IIM C

My estimates:
The panelists jugde you on various parameters ,and if they find out that you have a serious weakness in one of those parameters ,u are out.What i mean is that they prefer an allround personality who , even if he doesnt have a great strength in a particular area , doesnt have a debilitating weakness in some area.
Even the CAT tests this also I feel, and that is the reason for cutoffs, and they follow the policy in GD/PIs too.
Bieng an IITian heped , but I was seriously questioned why I left the job , took a good deal of convincing to drive home the fact the I am soooo serious about IIMs.
Sorry if this is all just common sense and have been repeated many times ...but couldnt resist posting the gyan I have acquired.
