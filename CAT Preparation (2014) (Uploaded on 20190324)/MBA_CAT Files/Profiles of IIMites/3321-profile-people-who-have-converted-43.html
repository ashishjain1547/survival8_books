By heaven, at last at last - I get the right to post in this thread. Spent so many hours reading the profiles here, just trying to figure out what it takes to be in an IIM. My profile here will give hope to quite a few with not so outstanding profiles. Here it goes -

Xth - 88%
XII - 78.7% (Both State Board, totally Bengali Medium)

B.tech in IT from unknown college (Institute of Engineering & management, Kolkata). CGPA - 8.01/10 (Nothing great, people easily get upwards of 8.5 in my university, toppers end up with 9.5+)

2.5 years of IT exp with CTS & IBM.

Extra-Currics - zilch

Previous CATs

CAT 2004 - 98.41 (VA 99.84, DI 92.94, QA 65.33 )
CAT 2005 - 98.73 (VA 95.05, DI 96.47, QA 96.43) - IIML & MDI calls, IIML not converted. Didn't bother about MDI.
CAT 2006 - 97.16 (VA 97.26, QA 95.45, DI 82.84) - SPJIMR call, again not bothered.

Finally CAT 2007 - 99.73 (VA 98.53, QA 97.2, DI 99.37) - ACLIK, MDI, SPJIMR, IIFT

GD/PIs -

K - Avg GD + Avg PI - Converted
I - Avg GD + Good PI - Converted
L - Excellent GD + very Good essay + boring PI (10 minutes of nothing) - Converted
C - Not so good ATM + Excellent GD + very Decent PI - Converted both PGDM & PGDCM
A - Pretty Good essay + No Idea (Hit Some Sixers, but got bowled quite a few times too) PI - Converted


GD/PI experiences -

IIMK - http://www.pagalguy.com/forum/985543-post20.html
IIMI - http://www.pagalguy.com/forum/1014168-post104.html
IIML - http://www.pagalguy.com/forum/1017832-post194.html
IIMC - http://www.pagalguy.com/forum/1038948-post113.html
IIMA - http://www.pagalguy.com/forum/1109794-post156.html


Finally, word for future aspirants - Work hard, but work smart. Be well informed, not only about the world, but also about the MBA entrance process. It helps for sure. CAT is generally easy, if one can put in regular smart work, with PASSION. GD/PI is different. It's so much subjective, it's futile trying to analyze GD/PIs. Just try to be yourself. Only idea I got from my converts - perhaps the profs can look through and see the desire within you, your desire to be a student again. That attitude is all I think. Otherwise, someone with my profile, converting the best B School in Asia Pacific, I still can't believe it.

Joining - WIMWI

Result :

With a lot of patience, some hard work, and some luck - I made it to I.



My Comment :

Although all the IIMs claim that they give massive weightage to your CAT score while giving final calls, trust me - they don't. Basically, that weightage is only to ensure that those SC/ST(and now /OBC) candidates who get abysmal scores on their CAT (and still get a call), and perform better in GD/PI than a GEN CAT student won't get a GEN CAT seat. This is just a measure to ensure that both qualitatively and quantitatively enough candidates come through these quotas. So if you have a 98.xx %ile and a call from any IIM for a GEN seat, just do your best in your GD/PI, and you'll have an equally bright chance of making it, as compared to a 99.xx %iler. Trust me, I made it.
