A dream come true - an opportunity to post on this sacred thread on Pagalguy!!! I have so badly wanted my rank to display IIM X, and today I have it.

X: 84.27 % ---
XII: 73.33 %---
BE: 62.52 % ---
Heres my CAT story
CAT 2005: Slogged my buttocks off, only to panic in the exam.
Result: DISASTER. 94.33 %ile. My panicking cost me at least 12 marks, which would have meant that I would have had 99%ile +. Moral of the story: Change in format or not, chill

CAT 2006: Hardly any prep. Max three weeks. Borrowed material from my friend and solved papers.
Result:99.13%ile

Calls IIMC and IIM I

Converts: IIMC

I have pathetic grades when compared to most people here. Yet, I am in one of the finest MBA institutes in the country. What brings me here? I guess the belief that I could make it, and some hard work prior to CAT. Most people can get here. But they lose themselves in the mire of percentiles and mocks.

As regards to gyaan on interviews and GD/PI, I would say that one remains to be true to himself in the PI. Relax and enjoy the interaction with the IIM profs. Its something not many people get. The GD is a lottery actually, and depends on the group. Just dont be too aggressive.

Its great to have posted on this thread. All the very best to each and every one of you.

Cheers.
