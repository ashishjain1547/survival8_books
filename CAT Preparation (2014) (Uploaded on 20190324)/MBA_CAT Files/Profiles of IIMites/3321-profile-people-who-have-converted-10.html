Hi Guys,

Now that all the results are out, here's my profile

Prasanna Santhanam

CAT : 99.82
VA : 99.53
QA : 99.50
DI : 93.98

Calls : ABCK
Converted : ABK

Grad: Final Year Electronics & Communication Engineering
Delhi College of Engineering
Aggregate Percentage till 7th Sem: 82% (Branch topper)

GD/PIs

A : GD was fantabulous (A+++) and PI was quite arbit - (B???) - In hindsight, now that I got selected, I must have done something to their liking.. Personally, I think it was the approach that they were looking for, rather than the content, coz I had plenty of I don't knows in my interview. But never once did I shy away from trying to answer a question, no matter how much it took me by surprise.

B : GD was excellent (A++) and PI was excellent (A++) - It was a very easygoing interview - humorous and without any painful questions. I was pretty sure of bagging this one

C : GD was Good (A) and PI was very good (A+) - The PI was a kinda stresser, with both the panelists repeatedly asking me for specific career goals and asking me not to use any jargon terms. I was asked a lot of questions from my hobbies.

K : GD okayish (A-) and PI good (A) - The K GD was an abstract topic, yet very noisy. The interview was a cool affair.

On the whole, I was hardly asked any tech in my interviews.



So that was my Road to IIM 2005 Story. It has come to a happy ending.


All the best for the next year aspirants!!

Prasanna
