An honour to post in this thread!


My BG:

X std: 87%
XII std: 89%
BE (ECE): 76%
Work ex: Zilch

CAT score 99.18
DI/LA 95.99
Quant 98.05
Verbal 97.11


GD/PI calls: A,C,L

Analysis:

IIMA - Very good GD. Started it and contributed substantially. Give myself 7/10.
Good PI. Diverse topics. Was able to control the i/v. Very pleasant exp. 7/10
I guess my CAT score put me off the race even before the GD!

IIMC: Very good GD. Lots of points. One of my best GDs. 8/10
Excellent PI. Easily the best i/v i've ever given. My rating 9/10. 25 min. eclectic topics ranging from Spread spectrum technologies to writing styles of authors to cricket! Had a fantastic time. Never once did i stammer or search for words. Smooth sailing. The panel was very impressed with me. And I had a decent profile as well - scholarships, extra-currics etc. So was very hopeful of converting. But alas it wasnt to be!

IIML: Average GD. Wasnt at my best. Rating 5/10
Mediocre PI. Acad-based. Not much scope for me to control. Direct questions.. more like rapid-fire. Rating 6/10. Lasted just 15 mins. Came out with a bad feeling.

Well, apparently my defns of good/bad are incorrect cause A & C rejected me while IIML selected me albeit after a WL.

Personally, I think CAT score matters a lot. Though it is not the only thing, a low CAT score can prove to be a huge drawback (as was in my case. Else I'm sure I would've converted IIMC).

I'm off to Lucknow now. But I'm more convinced than ever that there is definitely something called luck!

Good luck to all IIM aspirants.

Life is what happens while you are too busy making other plans - John Lennon

FMS : In n out in a flash. 4mins interview, and a GD which looked like we were the usual A.P Assembly . 15 chaps to speak in 10mins abt an abstract topic.

Lastly, my IIM-I call came in the 2nd or 3rd list, don't even know which one... the letter just arrived home . IIM-K was in the final list, I was initially waitlisted.
