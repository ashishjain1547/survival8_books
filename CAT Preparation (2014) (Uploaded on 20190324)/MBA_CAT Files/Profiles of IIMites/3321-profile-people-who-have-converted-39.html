Better late than never

Fresher from Govt. Model Engineering College, Kochi.
Acads
B.Tech: 70.1%
12th: 75%
10th: 90.2%

Got 99.94 in CAT 2006,
sectionals - Verbal - 99.79, DI - 99.73, Quant - 98.5

Calls from LACKI. B took a loong look at my acads

Interview/GD details
1. K - GD was topic-based. About farmer suicides.
Chill interview. Only about my interests(football n Quizzing). Zero acads
2. I - Case-based GD.
During interview, grilled me a bit on acads. Could answer only around 60 pc.
Even then was confident throughout.
3. L - Cant remember GD. Interview lasted only 3 minutes. Got the impression that they were banking a lot on the first impression.
4. C - Case-based GD. This was the most enjoyable interview of the lot. Plenty of qs about football, barely any acads.
5. A - Case-based GD. Another enjoyable interview. I was able to crack a few jokes . Again, zero to minimal acads

Converted all 5. And chose IIMA.

Generally, I found that the IIM panels are looking for pleasant people who can have a normal conversation with them. Would help if you are confident and has an optimistic nature. Try to steer the interview towards whatever topic ur comfortable with.

All the best!

A word of advice for candidates: I feel I did pretty well in other GD/PI's too converting all but IIMA n waitlisted--> converted in NITIE [in spite of a horrible GD]. So my 2 pence for GD/PI. I feel[standard disclaimers apply] that there are certain things that the panel are looking for in a candidate.
- A basic knowledge of wat one has done. They won't expect rocket science.
- Good communication skills including listening skills in a GD.
- clearity of thought, a candidates thought process is demonstrated both in GD n PI.
- A Nice n likable personality. It pays to smile and be confident [I know thats tough considering the stakes]. N don't murder people during GDs.
- Strong morals and a positive attitude.
- Presence of mind. It makes more sense to not answer to answer wrongly. Don't make wild guesses.
- Clearity of GOAL.[why MBA? 5 years hence...].
- Be genuine. Just show them that u r interested in being a student.
- Sometimes stress handling skills..
Preparation is important. So if u are working in company X and don't know who is the CEO/ what fields X is into, it would be a negative. I personally dug up my companies website and was asked abt it frequently. I guess it helped.

Results: It did.
