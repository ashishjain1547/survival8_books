GD/INTERVIEW EXPERIENCE :

IIM - A : - Ok GD, Average Interview
Very good experience, the case study was good and I almost started the discussion. Contributed effectively. Interview was well structured and did well in technical questions.

IIM - C : - Ordinary GD, Relaxed Interview
The GD offered me not much to speak, I will not hesitate in terming the GD to be a case of group rejection but the Interview was very chilled out and relaxed. Discussed about how to design a house for a blind person with BAMBOO's.

IIM - L : - Awesome GD, Good Interview
The best GD I have ever attended, everyone agreed to everyone. The Interview hovered around books I have read so far. Aced the Job part.

IIM - I : - Good GD, Tensed Interview
My first experience. GD was confident display of knowledge with regular inputs to the discussion. PI was all about Extra Curricular Activities. On the spot offer.

IIM - K : - Good GD, Awesome Interview
I have never seen 3 spelling mistakes in 4 lines, well I saw it that day. GD was okayish, with everybody contributing, I would rate myself as the best performer in the group. PI was chilled with description of exp's in US of A.

After all the exp I feel GD/PI can be utilised for on the spot offers. Profs look for body language and thought process. I feel I was tending to arrogant during C interview. Speech delievery is also one important aspect. You should not be too loud, or sound arrogant.

Looking forward to IIM - L now

The most sacred thread, and its a dream come true to post here...

My profile:
CAT: 99.95 (overall)

Job: Business Analyst (11 months)

BE: DCE (2006)
XII: 80%
X: 88%

Calls: ACLIK
Converts: ACLIK

Finally going to WIMWI


My take on GDPIs:
A: Great GD, Good PI. I lead the entire interview here, and was tested on almost everything from acads to job ex. I owe this one to my work ex, and the personal data form. Spent almost 50 hours on that, and the profs actually read it pretty thoroughly.
C: Awesome GD, Excellent PI. The interviewers were amazingly laid back, and very impressive. Probably the most impressive panel ever! The whole interview was about my job, Khushwant singh , and cricket... and they were somehow also very interested in my name...
L: Excellent GD and PI. the whole interview again about my job. went into great depths and details about my job profile.
I: Good GD, average PI. The profs were very very cool. A long discussion about movies, family and some acads. managed to goof up in some places.
K: Poor GD, average PI. The GD was a fishmarket. and this was the only stress interview. The profs just wouldnt let me lead them. as soon as they saw that i was comfortable in one area, they would change the direction. The longest interview, and covered almost everything under the sun. I was even asked: why the smirk on your face?

My take on the overall process: A and C give you great scope to lead the interview. So if you dont make it, i dont think anyone else can be blamed. One thing that was constant in all my interviews was grilling on my job profile. A lot of hypothetical situations created, and contingency sought for.

Overall, the whole thing couldnt have been better

whats worse...
not being judged fairly, or not being judged at all
i wonder...
