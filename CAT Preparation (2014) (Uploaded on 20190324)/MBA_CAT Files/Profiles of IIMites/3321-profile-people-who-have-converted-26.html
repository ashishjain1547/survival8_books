Hi All,
Now that I have overcome my IIMA debacle ,i guess its time to post my profile & Experience summary for the benefit of future IIM aspirants..

Cat 2003 : 85.xx ( no IIM Calls)
CaT 2004 : 91.xx ( no IIM Calls)
CAT 2005 : 99.39 (BLACKI)
---------- verbal: 98.9 Quant: 94.56 DI: 96.01

X: 82.6 (CBSE)
XII: 84.2 (CBSE)
BE: 75.4 (N Guj Univ) -- Elec & Comm
Extra Curri: very Good @ School ; Decent @ College
********* All India NDA- SSB 43rd, Jan 2000 course ********
Work Ex: Total ~34 months
----Masibus, Gandhinagar : 7 months
----Wipro, Bangalore : 27 months

Converts: C, L, K,I, NITIE
Rejects: A,B, MDI..
No Calls from : SPJIMR

GD PI Experience Summary:
(scale: Pathetic- BAD- OK - GOOD- V Good )
A: Noisy GD, but OK ; Good PI : Expected call!
B: OK GD, Good PI : Expected call!
C: V Good GD, Bad PI : Never counted it as a chance!
but I guess I made it 'coz of my GD! ( joining JOKA!)
L: Good GD, Good PI : Expected call ( WL #63-- so thru i guess!)
K: OK GD( barely spoke, 1st GD of season) , Good PI : Was not sure!
I: OK GD, Pathetic PI : Expected a sureshot kick!

NITIE: Fish Market GD( me Good ), short 'n sweet PI (6 mins) : #13th on merit
MDI: OK GD, BAD PI : Expected kick

Well Frens!
after this successful season My overall feeling is that the selection process needs imporvement, It is not fool proof.. A lot remains desired from these prestigious Institutes.. I'm sure in every batch from each college we'll find at least 20% students who cud be exchanged for more deserving ones who were left out.. but then if u were to ask me for a more better process, i won't be able to suggest any... but then thats not my job! I'm there to give my best and hope people see my best...
I feel the LUCK factor plays a major role during the selection process.. If the Lady Luck smiles on u and the PROFs perceive u as a HEERA , u r in or else out! Do I have to be SO dependent on LUCK to make it to these prestigious institutes! If at the end of those 20-25 mins the PROFs are not able to find any worthwhile elements in me ' coz they were focusing too much on my weak areas, whose fault is it anyways??

I made it to the institutes I expected a kick from and was left out from the ones I thought I was in.. The loss happen at the more important Battle fields! Atleast as a student I know where I'll definitely not make it & where I Surely will.. Then u have those in betweens! But having made it so far , i believe we have the intelligence to make out the SURESHOT ones.. but ALAS!!!!!!!!!!!
I know I'll be cribbing for the rest of my life 'coz i came so close to convertingIIMA! IIMA was my dream college, may be beacuse i spent so much time @A'bad and then fell in love with the campus..! I'll always wonder why the Profs didnt see THAT thing in me which the PROFS from other 4 IIMS could! afterall , i can't be 2 different person on 2 different days! I still believe I HAVE what it takes to be IIMA , but shall not try again & will join IIMC... But the dream shall remain unfulfilled...
I waited for IIMA all these years, and now IIMA shall have to wait for me, FOREVER!

If some of u think thats attitude, well then ... IT IS! u need some to be there!!!!

Disclaimer: Views expressed here are entirely mine. Pls dont make an issue of the contents here.. u may PM me if the urge is irresistable!
